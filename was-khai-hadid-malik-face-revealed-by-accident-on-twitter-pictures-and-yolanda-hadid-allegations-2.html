Was Khai Hadid Malik Face Revealed By Accident On Twitter? Pictures And Yolanda Hadid Allegations

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 16, 2024

Khai Hadid Malik’s face was accidentally posted on Twitter by her mother Gigi Hadid back in March 2021, she immediately deleted the picture after realizing the mistake. 

Khai Hadid Malik is the daughter of famous singer Zayn Malik and supermodel Gigi Hadid. She has been a keen interest of the people on the web after her parents separated recently. The couple announced their separation recently according to Times Of India.

The singer and supermodel had been dating for over two years now and share a daughter together. However, they recently revealed they will not be continuing their relationship any further but will co-parent their daughter.

Let us learn more about Khai Hadid Malik’s face reveal accident and Yolanda Hadid Allegations.

Was Khai Hadid Malik Face Revealed By Accident On Twitter?

Khai Hadid Malik’s face was accidentally revealed on Twitter by her mother Gigi Hadid.

She posted the face of Khai on Twitter back in March 2021 but deleted the image immediately after she realized her mistake.

Gigi’s fans and followers rallied around her and requested the internet users not to share the images any further to maintain the child’s privacy.

The supermodel might have tried to post the picture to her close circle but was accidentally posted to the public. Or it could be a total accident, she never clarified the situation on the web.

Gigi often posted pictures with her daughter on her social media accounts and flaunted the mother-daughter day out but never had she made the child’s face public on the web reports Capital FM.

Did Yolanda Hadid Leaked Khai Pictures?

No, Yolanda Hadid, grandmother of Khai was not responsible for leaking Khai’s face pictures on the internet.

She has recently posted several pictures of Khai on her Instagram account wishing the little angel her first birthday.

However, in the pictures, Yolanda shared with her followers on her picture-sharing social media platform Khai’s face is not seen. She captioned the pictures as, “Happy first birthday to our Angel Khai…., No words can express how much love and joy you have brought into our lives in just one year…”

Khai is seen bonding with her grandmother in the pictures and riding a little pony but her face has been placed under curtains on the series of images.

What Are Yolanda Hadid Allegations Against Zyan Malik?

Recently internet burned after Yolanda Hadid alleged Zyan Malik of hitting her.

According to an article published by TMZ, Yolanda Hadid claimed she was struck by Zyan Malik.

However, the singer has denied the allegations of striking his then-girlfriend’s mother Yolanda according to Yahoo News.

Yolanda was considering filing an official report but the information about her moving forward with the idea has not come to the surface yet.

Zyan Malik And Gigi Hadid Split Explored

Zyan Malik and Gigi Hadid have parted their ways recently according to various sources.

However, the ex-couple will continue to co-parent their daughter Khai for her better future.

The ex-couple have not discussed the reason for their separation with their fans and followers as of now but they could have their own differences.

They have urged their fans and followers to give them privacy and respect them at this point in time according to India Today.
