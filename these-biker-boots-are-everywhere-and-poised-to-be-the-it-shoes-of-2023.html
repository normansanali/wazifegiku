Miu Mius Sienna biker boots are everywhere: Find out why they are set to be the It shoes of 2023

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Miu Miu has done it again, delivering yet another must-have item of the season.

After the mini-skirt craze the brand induced back in 2022, they have been dominating our minds, feeds and the wardrobes of every It-girl worth her publicist recently thanks to the Italian fashion brand’s cult-adored Sienna boots. 

“The most stylish It girls have been seen wearing them on and off the runway,” explains Shivani Dhillon, founder of Wardrobe Couture. It’s no surprise that requests have been flooding in for the nearly sold-out Sienna boots Shivani says, because “they are a mix of quirky and tough, you’ll be able to wear them forever.”

Shivani has made a name for herself as the go-to person for finding those impossible to source pieces. From vintage Chanel bags to archival Hermes, she tracks pieces down for the world's biggest influencers and A-listers, and her clients include supermodels such as Elsa Hosk and household name influencers.

“It’s funny as Elsa Hosk and Pernille Teisbaek sent their Miu Miu requests a week apart from each other. The two ladies have impeccable style and always come to me for the hardest of finds,” explains Shivani.

She also sourced a pair for Kylie Jenner's BFF Anastasia Karanikolaou known online as Stassie Baby, “She had been looking everywhere for them, when your celebrity clients ask you to find an item they are unable to locate themselves you can almost guarantee it’s going to be a difficult find and no walk in the park,” Shivani tells us, “But within 24 hours I located them, shipped them and delivered them to her home within 7 days of the request.”

“When the boots launched on the runway, the amount of requests that we received—be it a pre-order or anyone trying to get their hands on these boots—went crazy” Shivani says, “Usually we see a trend in a particular shoe or boot and after a month or so we are onto the next, however since their release last year, the sold-out boots have been seen on many celebrities and influencers… and the boots are proving stronger than ever this year.”

How do I style my biker boots?

“These boots are so versatile they have been styled so many ways, Elsa styled hers with tight blue jeans, bandana and oversized vintage leather biker jacket, Pernille styled hers with a mini skirt and oversized Miu Miu biker jacket,” explains Shivani. 

“On the other hand ‘Stassie’ has just worn her boots to Coachella and has styled them with denim shorts and a crop top. Kourney Kardashian has just been seen wearing similar biker boots, pairing hers with an oversized black and blue faux fur coat, hoodie and shorts.”

But just why are the Miu Miu boots proving so popular with our favourite It-girls? Shivani has a theory, “Boots make a bold statement, and a solid pair of boots is always worth investing in, and can be pulled out year after year."
