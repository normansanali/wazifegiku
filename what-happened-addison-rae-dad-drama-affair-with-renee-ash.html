Addison Rae's Dad Changed Their Relationship After His Affair With A Young Model

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

There's been a lot of turmoil in TikTok superstar Addison Rae's life. This is due to many things involving her father Monty Lopez. The main cause of the turmoil stems from Monty having a secret relationship with a 25- year-old model. Ever since the rumors started, her family has fallen apart in ways the superstar never expected.

On top of that, all of this trouble has caused her individual family members to gain even more clout. At this point, Addison's family might be more famous than her.

This includes Monty, who become a star on Instagram and TikTok in his own right. It all started with that secret relationship. Ever since then he's posted a lot of pictures and videos on the numerous platforms he's on. Some of those posts have been directed at certain family members. Some have been indirectly about the incident itself.

Here's everything to know about the incident.

6 Who Is Monty Lopez?

Monty Lopez was born on November 24th, 1975 in Louisiana. The Bayou state would be where he would meet his wife Sheri Easterling. It's where Monty has spent most of his life.

Louisiana also happens to be the birthplace of Addison. She was born on October 6th, 2000 in Lafayette, and lived there until December 2019. That was when she moved into the Hype House in Los Angeles and became friends with Kourtney Kardashian. The rest is history.

Related: 10 Facts About Addison Rae's Family And Life

Like all parents, Monty and Sheri had a huge effect on Addison when she was growing up. One of the biggest impacts include their on and off relationship. They were separated for a long period of her childhood. Aside from that, not much has been revealed about Addison's family when it comes to her upbringing.

5 Monty's Secret Affair With Young Model Renee Ash

Things seemed fine in Addison's family life up until the scandalous events that happened earlier this year.

The 46-year-old Monty reportedly "misled" a 25-year-old model named Renee Ash into a relationship. He did this by telling her that the relationship between him and Sheri was over. At the time of the affair, though, everything was seemingly fine in their marriage. Once the affair came to light, this did cause Monty and Sheri to separate. All of this was first reported by Renee herself when she posted videos on an anonymous TikTok account.

4 Who Is Renee Ash?

Renee is a social media star just like Addison. She's most famous on Instagram, where she has over 40,000 followers.

There were rumors that Monty was cheating for a while before Monty and Renee were spotted out in public together. After it became public that the relationship was in fact an affair, she spilled the tea with Page Six. She claimed that he had told her multiple times he was in the process of getting a divorce. At one point, she even mentioned that she thought he got her pregnant.

In the end, Renee felt bad about what happened to Addison and Sheri. Renee was a victim of a lie and left Monty after the truth was revealed.

3 What Monty Thinks Of His Affair

Monty has responded to the mess he's made primarily by posting on TikTok and other social media platforms. He posted a video of himself lip-syncing a clip where he pondered whether he was the villain or not.

What makes things even worse is that more videos have come out since Renee went public about the affair. Some of them were allegedly taken before him and Sheri were separated. Some videos and pictures have come out that were taken afterward as well. At this point, Monty himself has even posted pictures of him with younger women.

2 How Addison Rae Feels About Her Father

Addison Rae might be rich and famous, but that doesn't mean she doesn't have to deal with family problems.

Addison hasn't said much explicitly about her dad's affairs, but sometimes actions speak louder than words.

Monty was also spotted in a screenshot on facetime talking to a 19-year-old girl. This prompted Addison and Sheri to unfollow him on all social media accounts.

Addison hasn't let this affect her success at all. She's still one of the most famous and influential young stars in the world.

1 Everything To Know About Sheri Easterling and Yung Gravy's Relationship

Perhaps the most interesting thing about this entire affair has been that Sheri entered a relationship with rapper Yung Gravy.

After Monty's relationship with Renee became public, Sheri denied his requests for getting back together. She's bashed her ex-husband almost every time she's been asked about him and their relationship.

Monty has responded to their relationship by asking Yung Gravy to fight him in a boxing fight. He's also repeatedly called Sheri "leftovers" on social media. Monty usually posts on Instagram Stories, where he's thrown a lot of shade at both his ex and the internet-based rapper. Yung Gravy has clapped back at him by calling him an "embarrassment" of a father. At this point in time, however, he has not accepted any request to fight in a boxing match.
