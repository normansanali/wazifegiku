Hailey Bieber's Long Black Dress Features a Thigh-High Leg Slit

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Having captured our intention in a Magda Butrym leather suit earlier this week, Hailey Bieber proved, once again, that she's completely over sweatpants by wearing a sleek black cut-out dress with a thigh-high leg slit out to dinner on Friday.

Stepping out with her husband, Justin Bieber, who opted for a fluorescent pink beanie hat, Hailey kept date night simple yet chic in an all-black ensemble. The model wore a floor-length black dress featuring a high slit, and a long-sleeve crop top, revealing her midriff. Per Instagram account @haileyrbiebercloset, Hailey wore The Andamane's Gia dress, and carried Bottega Veneta's woven pouch bag.

She accessorized her look with Anita Ko's Zoe necklace, and Shay Jewelry's Diamond Infinity Y necklace. Hailey also wore Saint Laurent's peep-toe mink-covered stiletto mules, which retail for $1,095.

This week, Justin announced that his new album, Justice, will be released on March 19. On Instagram, he revealed, "In a time when there’s so much wrong with this broken planet we all crave healing and justice for humanity. In creating this album my goal is to make music that will provide comfort, to make songs that people can relate to and connect to so they feel less alone. Suffering, injustice and pain can leave people feeling helpless. Music is a great way of reminding each other that we aren’t alone. Music can be a way to relate to one another and connect with one another."
