Coco Arquette Facts

  • Her parents’ divorce finalized in the year 2013 and they later maintained a friendly relationship.
  • Coco Arquette made her first theatrical film appearance by playing the role of Vickie and Albert’s daughter in the 2014 black comedy-drama film, Just Before I Go, which starred the likes of Seann William Scott, Elisha Cuthbert, Olivia Thirlby, Garret Dillahunt, and Kate Walsh. The film was directed by her mother Courteney Cox and Coco Arquette’s role in the film went uncredited.
  • Her biological father played the role of her on-screen father in Just Before I Go.
  • Coco Arquette has also appeared in a couple of TV shows which include VH1: All Access, Entertainment Tonight, and CNN Newsroom.
  • She enjoys spending time with her friends.
