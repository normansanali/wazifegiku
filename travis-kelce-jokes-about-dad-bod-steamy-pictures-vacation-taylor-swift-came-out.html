Travis Kelce Jokes About His 'Dad Bod' After Steamy Pictures Of His Vacation With Taylor Swift Came

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Highlights

  • Travis Kelce addressed comments about his "dad bod" in a lighthearted manner on his podcast.
  • The Kelce brothers and Saquon Barkley joke about weight in a recent episode of "New Heights".
  • While Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift are happy together, they haven't announced any engagement plans just yet.

Travis Kelce and his brother Jason are known for having fun on their podcast "New Heights", and even when they talk about serious things, it's always lighthearted and humorous. As fans know, Travis and Taylor Swift jetted off to the Bahamas, making the most of their downtime together before their busy schedules resume.

How Travis Kelce Handles The Pressures That Come With Dating Taylor Swift

Travis Kelce appears to be well-prepared to handle the pressures that come with dating a superstar like Taylor Swift.

On his return, he spoke with his brother and their guest Saquon Barkley about his weight gain, making fun of the comments about the pictures of their vacation, where fans had pointed out that he was sporting a "dad bod".

Travis Kelce Defended His 'Dad Bod' In His And Jason Kelce's Podcast

The Kelce brothers talk about pretty much everything in their podcast "New Heights", and in the latest episode they addressed the comments about Travis Kelce's "dad bod."

The Chiefs star and Taylor Swift went on a romantic getaway to the Bahamas and some pictures were leaked of the couple at the beach. The two of them were all over each other in the steamy photos, and while fans celebrated how happy they looked, there were also a few comments about Travis Kelce's "dad bod." The remarks weren't malicious, but they stood out, and the NFL player responded to them with good humor.

On their podcast, Jason Kelce and Travis had new Philadelphia Eagles running back Saquon Barkley as a guest, and the three of them joked about their weight.

When Saquon Barkley asked the brothers who would win in a drinking game between the two of them, Jason quickly replied saying he was bigger. "I can definitely drink more volume, I don't even think that's a question."

To that, Travis replied that the retired player was "already down to 260", but Jason corrected him saying he actually weighed 283 pounds. That's when Travis referenced his weight gain in the off season, saying "We're in the same weight class now. It's March! We're in the same weight class right now."

They made one more reference to Travis and Taylor when Saquon Barkley talked about wanting to be "the next Apollo Creed". Jason Kelce said to him to just "throw it out there" because "stuff happens when it goes out on New Heights". He was, of course, referring to the time Travis Kelce mentioned he wanted to give Taylor Swift his phone number in a friendship bracelet. The rest, of course, is history.

Jason Kelce Warned Travis About Engagement Rumors On The Podcast

While Jason Kelce encouraged Saquon Barkley to manifest things in "New Heights", he also warned Travis when, a week ago, he made a comment that fueled rumors that he was about to propose to Taylor Swift.

They were talking about NBA star Victor Wembanyama, who is over 7 feet tall, and the Chiefs star said "You can't tell me that dude wasn't made in a f**king lab over there in France." Jason nodded along, but then Travis added"Lab-grown diamonds, that’s a lab-grown f**king NBA player. Can't wait til I f**kin’ make one," and the older brother groaned.
