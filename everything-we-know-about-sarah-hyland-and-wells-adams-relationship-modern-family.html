Everything We Know About Sarah Hyland And Wells Adams' Relationship

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, September 14, 2024

We all know Sarah Hyland as Haley Dunphy on the hit TV show, Modern Family, and we know Wells Adams from The Bachelorette and Bachelor In Paradise. Wells might’ve been unlucky in love on The Bachelorette and Bachelor In Paradise, however, he seems to have hit the jackpot with Sarah Hyland. The two met on social media, and ever since then, they’ve had quite the whirlwind romance. They fell in love fast and they fell hard for each other, and as a result, they’re the most adorable couple.

They’re very open about their relationship and aren’t afraid to share all of the adorable selfies and their cutest moments together with the entire world. It’s hard not to love their love, and you can’t help but root for them.

This is everything we know about Sarah Hyland and Wells Adams’ relationship.

15 Sarah Tweeted About Wells When He Was On The Bachelorette 

The whole thing started in 2016 when Sarah was watching The Bachelorette season that Wells was on with JoJo Fletcher. Sarah made it no secret that she had a crush on her future fiance, tweeting: "Ugh! @WellsAdams making a Lord of the Flies reference on men tell all??? #knewhewasthebestfromthestart #menwhoreadarehot.”

14 They Flirted On Social Media

In 2017, things started to heat up on social media between the two when Sarah tweeted her excitement to see Wells on Bachelor in Paradise as the bartender. She tweeted: “OH MY GOD @WellsAdams IS THE FRIKKIN BARTENDER?! Best. Season. Ever. #BachelorInParadise.”

She finally got his attention (and his reply) when he tweeted back “Taking applications for #BIP5 bar backs now. Please send references..Preferably from Phil Dunphy. I’m kidding the job is yours @Sarah_Hyland.”

In which Sarah responded: “I’ll start working on my garnishing skills. Oh and I require 35% of your tips… or free dogs.” That was only the beginning of their flirting.

13 He Slid Into Her DMs

After the flirting back and forth on Twitter, Wells decided to take a step further and slide into Sarah’s DMs on Twitter. Sarah recently revealed that what he messaged her was sweet, simple, and to the point which made her like him that much more. He said: “Next time I’m in LA, I’m taking you out for drinks and tacos.”

12 He Was By Her Side During Her Kidney Transplants

In September 2017, Sarah had to have a second kidney transplant. It was also around the same time that Sarah met Wells in person. Three days before her surgery, the two met, and they were both smitten from the start. Wells stood by Sarah’s side before, during, and after her transplant, despite the fact that they weren’t officially dating yet, and barely knew each other.

11 Rumors Began Swirling When They Posted A Couples Costume On Halloween

About a month later, the two started to spark rumors that something was truly going on between them. Sarah posted a picture of them in their Halloween costume to her Instagram. They went as a couples costume, Sarah dressing as Dustin and Wells dressing as Eleven from the hit Netflix show, Stranger Things.

10 It Was Reported They Were Dating In November Of 2017

After that Halloween photo, and posting a few flirty photos in between, the rumor mill really started swirling. In November of 2017, most, if not all of the world’s tabloids started to report that the two of them were officially dating. And for once, despite speculation, they were right about Wells and Sarah.

9 They Spent Major Holidays Together

Despite the fact that they had only been together for a few months, they took another huge step together, spending major holidays together. For Christmas, Sarah and Wells spent it together with Wells’ family, the two of them sharing the cutest selfies and pictures on social media to share with the world.

8 He Was By Her Side During Her Kidney Scare

In 2018, Sarah had another health scare related to her kidney issues, which led her to go to the hospital. Although Wells was away for work, he made sure that he was still there for Sarah by any means possible, and tried to make it back home to her as fast as he could.

7 They Did Long Distance For A While

Unfortunately for the couple, they were forced to do long-distance for a little while during their relationship. Of course, Sarah lived in LA since she was taping episodes of Modern Family, however, Wells lived in Nashville at the time. They made it work, though, as Wells flew to see her almost every weekend.

Related: 14 Pics Showing How Much Sarah Hyland Has Changed Since Season 1 Of Modern Family

6 They Moved In Together Summer 2018

During the summer of 2018, Wells and Sarah took another big step in their relationship, as the two moved in together just shy of a year of dating. Rumors swirled when a moving truck was spotted in front of Sarah’s house in Los Angeles. The rumors were true, as the two have been living together since.

5 They're Super Cute On Social Media

Love them or hate them, there’s no denying that Wells and Sarah and definitely one of the cutest couples in Hollywood. On social media they can be rather adorable, posting selfies and cute photos of one another. They’re extra sappy when they celebrate holidays and milestones, and we can’t help but love them!

4 Wells Asked Julie Bowen For Permission To Marry Sarah

Since 2009, Julie Bowen has played Sarah’s mom, Claire Dunphy on Modern Family. As a result, she’s been a second mother to Sarah over the years. Before Wells proposed to Sarah, he decided that he should also ask Sarah’s TV mom. Julie was thrilled and really liked Wells from the beginning, and was more than happy to give them her blessing.

Related: Modern Family's Haley Dunphy: 16 Things To Know About Sarah Hyland

3 Their Parents Haven't Met

It may come as a surprise, however, Wells’ parents and Sarah’s parents still haven’t met yet, and they probably won’t actually meet until the two get married. Why is that? They both fall on completely opposite sides of the political spectrum. Sarah’s family is very liberal while Wells’ family are extremely conservative, so they’re dreading the meeting between them.

2 They Got Engaged In July 2019

On July 16, 2019, Wells finally popped the question. He and Sarah were on a super romantic in Fiji, and when they were enjoying some time on the beach, he got down on one knee and asked her the big question, and of course, she said yes! Wells also managed to capture the whole thing with a photographer and a drone.

1 They're Not Rushing To Plan Their Wedding

Unfortunately for Sarah and Wells, they really don’t have any plans set in stone for their wedding. They had talked about the possibility of having it soon, but with the recent pandemic turning everything upside down, they decided to put all planning on hold until they’re able to have the wedding of their dreams.
