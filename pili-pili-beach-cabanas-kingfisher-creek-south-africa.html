Pili Pili Beach Cabanas, kingfisher creek, South Africa

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, September 20, 2024
From here you can explore 30km of unspoilt beaches, our pristine estuary, or the bustling not-so-slow Slow Town of Sedgefield, situated midway between Knysna and George.
Whether you choose to kick back and relax or go out and grab it by the horns, with its natural splendor, action packed activities and great restaurants and markets we believe you will have an awesome stay.
Breakfast, lunch, dinner, great coffee and kiff cocktails all available from the PiliPili Beach Restaurant downstairs.

Address:

Pili Pili Beach Cabanas

2 Claude Urban Dr, Myoli Beach, Sedgefield, 6573, South Africa,
