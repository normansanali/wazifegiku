Bill Melugin Biography

Bill Melugin is an American multiple EMMY, Murrow, and Golden Mike award-winning Journalist who was born on February 26, 1985 and brought up in Orange County, California. Currently, he works at KTTV-TV FOX 11 Los Angeles in Santa Monica, California as an Evening Investigative Reporter. Prior to joining the KTTV-TV news team in June 2017, he worked at WJZY-TV (FOX O&O) in Charlotte, North Carolina as a News Anchor and Reporter.

Bill also worked at KFOX 14 in El Paso, Texas as a Lead Nightside Reporter and Fill-In Anchor. He worked as a Model at FORD/Robert Black Agency and at Wilhemina Models. He also worked at Cronkite News as a Reporter and Thursday Anchor and KNXV-TV ABC 15 in Phoenix, Arizona as a News Intern. He served as a Newscast Production as a Cronkite NewsWatch KAET TV 8.3 and as a Videographer at SunDevils101.
