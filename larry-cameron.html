Career

Larry Cameron grew up in Natchez, Mississippi. He played football in high school and college as a teenager. He was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the NFL in 1973 and later by the Canadian Football League, where he played for the BC Lions and Ottawa Rough Riders. He was an all-star in 1975 and 1976, and he helped Ottawa win the Grey Cup. Due to injuries, his football career would be cut short. Cameron became Mr. Minnesota and Northern States Bodybuilding champion after his football career ended before being discovered by Ed Sharkey and wrestling in his Promotion AWA.
