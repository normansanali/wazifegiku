Fanatics Secures Rights to English Premier League and the Impact on Sports Cards

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

In a groundbreaking development that has sent ripples through the world of sports memorabilia, Fanatics has announced a multi-year partnership with the English Premier League, marking a significant shift in the landscape of sports cards and collectibles.

Fanatics: The Game Changer

Fanatics, a prominent online retailer of licensed sports memorabilia, has solidified its international rights portfolio with this historic deal. This move signifies a further expansion of Fanatics' licensing agreements outside the U.S., adding to its existing partnerships with the Bundesliga and UEFA Club Competitions. The company's extensive reach in soccer has now been further bolstered by securing the exclusive rights to the English Premier League2.

The Impact on Soccer Cards

The implications of this partnership extend beyond the realm of soccer, reaching into the world of sports cards. With the commencement of this agreement in June 2025, Fanatics Collectibles will become the sole provider of Premier League trading cards, trading card games, and stickers. This means that renowned players such as Erling Haaland, Phil Foden, and Kevin De Bruyne, among others, will now feature on Topps cards and autographs, marking a significant shift away from Panini and the renowned Prizm cards.

Impact on Card Collectors

The impact of this monumental deal on card collectors and enthusiasts cannot be overstated. Collectors will witness a transformation in the availability and design of their beloved soccer cards. The transition from Panini to Topps will undoubtedly create a new dynamic in the world of sports card collecting. The exclusivity of Topps Chrome soccer cards is set to make them a highly sought-after commodity, adding a new layer of excitement for fans and collectors alike.

The Rise of Topps Chrome Soccer

With the shift from Panini to Topps, Topps Chrome soccer cards are poised to take center stage as a coveted item for fans looking to add these exclusive cards to their collections. The announcement has sparked anticipation and excitement among enthusiasts, as it opens up the possibility of chasing different numbered cards and parallels of their favorite players in the best products available, akin to the excitement surrounding Bowman Chrome in the baseball card world.

Conclusion

The acquisition of the English Premier League rights by Fanatics represents a significant milestone in the world of sports memorabilia. As fans and collectors eagerly await the arrival of these exclusive cards, the impact of this deal will undoubtedly redefine the sports card collecting landscape. This historic partnership between Fanatics and the English Premier League is set to inject a new level of enthusiasm and anticipation into the world of soccer and sports card collecting, making it an exciting time for enthusiasts worldwide.
