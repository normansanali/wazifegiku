Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 5, 2024

Young-aged Donny Carrington is an Actor who came under the spotlight after he was cast in ‘All the Queen’s Men’ on BET+.

Stuntman Donny Carrington is set to get a major role in BET+ All The Queen’s Men.

The series center around a businesswoman Marilyn ‘Madam’ DeVille, who owns a nightclub with trusted employees. Things go downhill when she realizes more money means more problems.

The series is set to air on the 9th of September 2021 on the BET + channel.

All The Queen’s Men: Donny Carrington Wikipedia

Donny Carrington is born as the son of Jaqueline Denise Carrington and Donny Rogers Carrington Sr. His younger years were spent playing every sport.

It was evident that he had superior athletic abilities than his peers. Couple that with his love of performing, his future was clear.

His path to Hollywood started when he joined the industry as a stunt performer in 2012.

After nearly a decade of crafting his skill and making his name know, he is finally set to star in his first major role.
