Bad Girls Club Star, Linsey &quot;Jade&quot; Berardi, Dead at 22

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 8, 2024

Bad Girls Club star Linsey “Jade” Berardi has died at the age of 22, Oxygen said in a statement on Friday, April 3. Berardi’s cause of death has not been released.

"We are deeply saddened by the loss of Linsey 'Jade' Berardi, who appeared in Bad Girls Club Season 12," an Oxygen Media spokesperson said in a statement posted to Oxygen’s site. "Our deepest sympathy and heartfelt thoughts go out to her family, friends and fans."

The reality star, who was known as “Brooklyn Brat” on the show, reportedly got kicked off the show after getting into a physical altercation with a cameramen.

Her costars were first to comment about her death earlier this week. Jada Cacchilli tweeted on April 2: “Sad to find out @misslinseyjade life was cut so short…appreciate the ones you keep close!!” Shortly after her first tweet, Cacchilli tweeted, “Drugs ruin so many lives…,” although the details surrounding Berardi’s death remain unclear.
