Kristen Stewart Splits With Girlfriend Soko

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, October 2, 2024

Calling it quits. Kristen Stewart and girlfriend Stephanie “Soko” Sokolinski have split after a few months of dating, Us Weekly can exclusively confirm.

While the couple are currently broken up, they may get back together in the future, a source tells Us. The Snow White and the Huntsman star, 26, and the French singer-songwriter, 30, were first spotted looking romantic on a March trip to Paris. For the last two months, the two women have frequently packed on the PDA while out and about in L.A. On Easter Sunday, Soko even sucked on the actress’ thumb while picking up takeout at a restaurant in the Los Feliz area of Los Angeles.

Prior to the split, Soko seemed to be pretty smitten with the Twilight star. “I’m very, very, very in love and very happy in a relationship,” she told W magazine in late March.

Speculation about their breakup began after Stewart was spotted hanging out with Miley Cyrus’ model ex Stella Maxwell. They both hit the official Met Gala afterparty at NYC’s Boom Boom Room on Monday, May 2. Although Stewart is single, a source tells Us there’s nothing going on between the two ladies. “They absolutely did not hook up,” the source says. “Stella has mutual friends with Kristen, and they just hung out a little at the party.”

Stewart, who is notoriously private, has refused to publicly discuss her relationship with Soko — but she did open up about her sexuality in an August 2015 interview with Nylon. “Google me, I’m not hiding,” she told the magazine. “I think in three or four years, there are going to be a whole lot more people who don’t think it’s necessary to figure out if you’re gay or straight. It’s like, just do your thing.”
