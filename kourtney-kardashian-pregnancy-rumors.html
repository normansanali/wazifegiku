Kourtney Kardashian Just Responded to Those Pregnancy Rumors

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 26, 2024

Currently, three members of the Kardashian-Jenner family are reportedly expecting a child. Kourtney Kardashian isn't one of them, but that didn't stop her from joking that she is.

The mother of three surprised a Grazia reporter in an interview when she quipped that she, too, is with child. The article begins: "'I'm pregnant,' declares Kourtney Kardashian, with all the gravitas of a newscaster announcing World War III."

However, the writer clarifies two paragraphs later: "She reassures me she's joking after an uncomfortably long pause."

Kourtney explained on Twitter yesterday that her quote was taken out of context. In fact, she was actually answering a question about the craziest rumor she's read about herself online.
