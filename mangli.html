Mangli Wiki/Biography

Born on 10 June 1994, Mangli’s age is 26 Years as of 2020. She was born and brought up in a middle-class family in Anantapur district, Andhra Pradesh, India.

Mangli Childhood Photo

She completed her early schooling at Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan’s International School, Andhra Pradesh. After that, she enrolled herself at Sri Venkateswara University, Andhra Pradesh from where she completed her diploma in Carnatic music.

Since childhood, she was inclined towards singing, and always wanted to become a music teacher. Her father and her teachers also encouraged her to become a singer from a very young age.

Career

Mangli started her career with her debut appearance as a guest artist on a Telugu news channel “V6 News” in 2013.

She was invited for a special show “Dhoom Dham” on the occasion of the Dasara festival and gained huge popularity after her appearance in this show.

After that, she appeared in the satirical news show “Teenmar News” which premiered on the “V6 News” channel in which she played the role of Maatakari Mangali was a big hit in her career.

She also worked with the “HMTV” news channel on the show “Jordar News”, and then she joined the web channel “MIC TV” where she works as an anchor and folk song singer.

She interviews many celebrities and also sings songs on many Telangana festivals like “Bathukamma”, “Bonalu”, “Telangana Formation Day”, and many other festivals. Her songs become huge hits on YouTube also and have millions of views.

She sang songs for Telugu movies like Sapthagiri Express, Raj Mahal, Needhi Naadi Oka Katha, George Reddy and Ala Vaikuntapurramlo.

Some of the famous songs are Jao Banjara Song, Shivaratri Song, Diwali Song, Dussera Song, Sankranti Song, Rela re Rela re, Sammakka Sarakka, etc. Every song of her has millions of views on YouTube.

She also participated in the TV show “Maharani” as a participant. Recently she participated in the TV reality show Bigg Boss Telugu 4.
