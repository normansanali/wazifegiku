Dua Saleh Ethnicity and Religion Age Height and Sexuality

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

Dua Saleh, a non-binary gender actor belongs to the Tunjur ethnicity who follows Islam religion. Learn more about her. 

According to Wikipedia, Saleh is a recording artist, songwriter, poet, and actor based in Minnesota, U.S.

Get To Know Dua Saleh Ethnicity- What Is Their Religion?

Dua Saleh is an American-Sudanese actor who belongs to the Tunjur ethnicity of Sudan and follows Islam religion. They were born in Kassala, Sudan, to the Tunjur heritage who are originally from Chad/Darfur.

The artist is Muslim but they once shared that they are not really religious and Ramadan is complicated for them. But the day before the Ramadan, they retweeted a clip of a five-times-daily call to prayer that was being broadcast from the roof of a local mosque.

They also woke up early to have the pre-dawn meal that is eaten before the fasting begins but they said that they tried to pray but their body did not feel like it.

They had a tough childhood because their family fled from the country because of the civil war when they were five years old. The family landed up in the United States, and after moving to different places they finally settled down in St.Paul’s neighborhood Rhonda.

The Muslim artist completed their high school study at Saint Paul Central High School. After finishing high school education, they joined Augsburg University and earned a double degree with a major in gender, women’s and sexuality studies and sociology in 2017.

Learn About Dua Saleh Age & Height

Dua Saleh is assumed to be around 25-30 years old and should have a height of more than 5ft 5in. Their birth date and height details have not been revealed yet.

Their family moved from Sudan in the 1990s when they were five years old, which explains that they could be in their late 20’s.

They have been interested in writing poems since they were four years old. They finally shared their poem in 2017 through a Youtube channel named Button Poetry. They also released their single debut in the same year.

They made an appearance on screen through a Netflix series named Sex Education which the audience has loved.

What is Dua Saleh Sexuality?

Dua Saleh defines their sexuality as Non-Binary who are neither male nor female. They are pronounced as (They/them).

They share that they found they came forward as non-binary gender when they were in their second year at Augsburg University. Nevertheless, they also claimed that they realized their gender a long time ago.

They have been involved in Neighbourhood’s Organizing for Change and Augsburg’s LGBTQIA student services.
