Hulk Hogan Latest: TNA Wrestling Begins Scrubbing Hogan, TMZ Says It Has Seen Footage of Hogans R

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 2, 2024

TNA Wrestling Begins Pulling Hulk Hogan

It looks like TNA has begun to scrub Hulk Hogan from its company platforms, as Hulk Hogan has been removed from the Alumni section of TNA’s website, and his merchandise has been pulled from TNA’s online shop.

TMZ Says It Has Seen Footage of Hogan’s Racist Rant

Earlier today, several readers sent in word that TMZ is claiming it has seen the footage of the tape which features the controversial Hulk Hogan racist rant.

The tape is the same tape from which The National Enquirer released audio, but the only thing TMZ has released thus far is a statement from Hogan’s attorney claiming Hogan resigned from WWE as opposed to being terminated, as WWE announced.
