Two Title Matches &amp; A Double Wedding Announced For WWE RAW Next Week

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 8, 2024

Tonight’s RAW took place inside Little Caesars Arena in Detroit, Michigan. WWE capped off the episode with a tag team match between SmackDown Tag Team Champions the Uso’s and the Street Profits.

Click here for our full coverage of Monday Night RAW.

WWE announced a couple of matches and a segment for next week’s RAW. The April 18th episode of the red brand will emanate from KeyBank Centre in Buffalo, New York.

On next week’s RAW, Theory (yes, just Theory) will take on WWE United States Champion Finn Bálor in a title match. Vince McMahon’s protégé looks to earn his first strap on the main roster.

WWE also announced another title for the April 18th episode of RAW. WWE Women’s Champions Sasha Banks and Naomi will defend their titles against Liv Morgan and Rhea Ripley.

Former WWE 24/7 Champion R-Truth will be officiating not one, but two weddings next week on RAW. Truth will officiate the weddings of Dana Brooke and Reggie and Akira Tozawa and Tamina Snuka.

  • Finn Bálor (c) vs. Theory – United States Championship Match
  • Sasha Banks and Naomi (c) vs. Liv Morgan and Rhea Ripley – Women’s Tag Team Championship Match
  • Double Wedding Segment
