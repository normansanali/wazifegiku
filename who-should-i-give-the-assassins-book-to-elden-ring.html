Who should I give the assassins book to Elden Ring?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 9, 2024

Who should I give the assassin’s book to in Elden Ring?

The assassin’s book in Elden Ring should be given to Brother Corhyn, the NPC wearing the Prophet class’ default armor near the Table. He will describe its heresy and then sell Darkness and Assassin’s Approach.

What to do with assassin book Elden Ring?

Collect the assassin’s prayerbook from behind the second Fog Wall. Return upstairs to Brother Corhyn, the NPC wearing the Prophet class’ default armor near the Table. Give him the Prayerbook and listen to him describe its heresy. He will then sell Darkness and Assassin’s Approach.

Does it matter who I give the Godskin Prayerbook to?

Yes, it matters who you give the Godskin Prayerbook to in Elden Ring. Giving it to Brother Corhyn or Miriel, Pastor of Vows, will unlock new incantations for purchase.

Should I give Corhyn the Two Fingers Prayerbook?

Yes, you should give the Two Fingers Prayerbook to Corhyn or Miriel. This will allow you to learn the “Lord’s Heal” and “Lord’s Aid” incantations from them, which are powerful healing spells that can restore both the player and nearby allies.

Who do I give the fire monks Prayerbook to?

You can give the Fire Monk’s Prayerbook to either Brother Corhyn or Miriel, the cleric tutors in Elden Ring. They will teach you the incantations associated with the Prayerbook.

Where should I give the Fire Monks Prayerbook?

You can give the Fire Monk’s Prayerbook to either Brother Corhyn in the main room of Roundtable Hold or to Miriel, Pastor of Vows, in Liurnia’s Church of Vows in Elden Ring.

Elden Ring: Where To Give Prayerbooks To Unlock Sorceries and Incantations

In Elden Ring, you need to give prayerbooks to specific NPCs to unlock sorceries and incantations. For example, give the Fire Monk’s Prayerbook to Brother Corhyn or Miriel to learn the associated incantations. Each NPC specializes in different types of magic, so choose wisely.

Should I give the Fire Monks Prayerbook to Brother Corhyn?

Yes, you should give the Fire Monks Prayerbook to Brother Corhyn in Elden Ring. He is one of the cleric tutors who can teach you the incantations associated with the Prayerbook. By giving it to him, you will be able to acquire new incantations for your character.

Is O Flame better than Catch Flame in Elden Ring?

O Flame is a much stronger version of Catch Flame in Elden Ring. However, O Flame is slower to cast and cannot be used out of jumps, rolls, or sprinting, which makes it less versatile compared to Catch Flame. Catch Flame, on the other hand, has a faster cast speed and can be used more easily in various situations.

Should I give Varre my finger in Elden Ring?

In Elden Ring, if you offer your finger to Varre during a conversation, you will receive the Bloody Finger item. Speaking to him again will give you a Pureblood Knight’s Medal, which grants you an audience with Luminary Mohg. It is up to you to decide whether to give Varre your finger or not, as it is not essential to the main story progression.

Is Brother Corhyn tarnished in Elden Ring?

Yes, Brother Corhyn is one of the many Tarnished NPCs in Elden Ring. He can be found at Roundtable Hold in the game’s opening hours, and he is a priestly figure who can teach players incantations.

What happens if I give Corhyn the Tonic of Forgetfulness in Elden Ring?

If you give Corhyn the Tonic of Forgetfulness in Elden Ring, he will remain at Stargazer’s Ruin until the end of the game and cannot be found despairing in Leyndell, Ashen Capital.
