Meet HGTV Chris And Calvin LaMont Parents: Patrick And Dawn Lamont!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Meet Chris and Calvin LaMont Parents: Patrick And Dawn Lamont.

The twin stars from HGTV have their hard work which allowed them to reach the height of success they are in today. But, along the way, they also had help from a few people including their parents. So, let us help our readers learn a few things about two people who made it possible for these successful Black men.

Below we attempt to unfold details on their age, career, and current residence. Read all about them here.

Who Are HGTV’s Chris And Calvin LaMont Parents?

Chris and Calvin LaMont, Dallas, Texas-based twin host from HGTV’s Buy It or Built It. They were born to their parents Patrick and Dawn LaMont on 16 May 1985. This means they are both 37 years old in 2022. As for their parents, they have been married for nearly four decades.

Both parents of Chris and Calvin thrived in a relationship as well as professional life. According to their respective Facebook, Patrick and Dawn shared their wedding vows on 20 August 1983. They are well-educated and hold prestigious jobs.

They have definitely succeeded as parents. Their twins Chris and Calvin went from owning and running a real estate company to starring in HGTV stars.

Since at least 2015, the twin brothers have been flipping houses. They relocated to Dallas in 2010, which coincidentally coincided with the commencement of their real estate careers. But, this was obviously not their first pick. The brothers started a janitorial and floor cleaning business when they first arrived in Texas.

Now fast forward to the present, and they already own over 25 residential lots. They’ve taken their construction firm to new heights, focusing on high-potential areas and getting the most passionate about up-and-coming districts.

Their show, Buy It or Built It, launched on May 18, 2022, and it focuses on assisting clients in deciding whether to purchase a home or build one from the ground up. They have a friendly competition to assist clients to decide whether to renovate an existing home or create a new one that meets their desires.

Meet HGTV Chris And Calvin LaMont Father, Patrick LaMont

Meet Patrick Donovan LaMont, father of Chris and Calvin LaMont from HGTV.

  • Patrick LaMont Age

In 2022, Patrick Lamont is 63 years old.

  • Patrick LaMont Job

Patrick LaMont graduated from Bishop Turner High School. After graduation, he enrolled at American InterContinental University which became AIU Atlanta after merging with AIU Dunwoody campuses in 2009. There, Patrick studied Business, Finance, and Health Care.

In 1996, Patrick graduated with an AA in Food Systems Management from Erie Community College. He secured his MBA in Healthcare Administration from AIU in 2008.

Patrick started his career as a General Manager at ARAMARK, Kingsbrook Jewish Medical Center where he worked for 6 years from 1991 to 1997. He also served as General Manager at ARAMARK CORPORATION from 1993 to 2000.

In addition, he also served as Food and Nutrition, Guest Services & Support Services in ARAMARK, University of Maryland Medical System from 1991 to 2000.

From 2000 to 2008, Patrick served as Food Service Director at Maimonides Medical Center. Whereas, he served as Steward at NSAME from 2000 to 2014. He is currently the Subject Matter Expert (SME) at Sidra Medical Research Centre.

  • Is Patrick LaMont On Instagram?

Indeed Patrick Lamont is on Instagram (@plamont161) where he hasn’t posted anything yet. He is also on Facebook (@patrick.lamont.54).

Meet HGTV Chris And Calvin LaMont Mother, Dawn LaMont

Dawn Marie LaMont is the beloved mother of HGTV’s Chris and Calvin LaMont.

  • Dawn LaMont Age

In August 2021, Dawn LaMont turned 60 years old. She celebrates her birthday on 6 August.

  • Dawn LaMont Job

Dawn LaMont is pursuing a career in health care. She is working as Patient Service Manager Health Care at Mt. Sinai-Hospital West.

  • Dawn LaMont On Instagram?

Yes, Dawn LaMont is on Instagram (@lamontdawn). She has two Facebook pages (@DawnLamont & @dawn.lamont.12).

Related FAQs

  • Are HGTV Chris And Calvin LaMont Parents Still Married?

Yes, it appears that HGTV’s Chris and Calvin LaMont’s parents are still happily married. Although they are both relatively less active on social media, Dawn posted in May 2021, “Love my hubby to the moon & beyond Dallas, Texas Real cowboy life Throw Back”.

  • How Many Kids Do HGTV Chris And Calvin LaMont Parents Have?

Besides the twin Chris and Calvin, Patrick and Dawn are parents to one more son, Wayne Lamont. Wayne is currently a resident of San Francisco, California. He attended and graduated from Piscataway Township High School and Grossmont College, San Diego. Find Wayne on Facebook (@wayne.lamont.54).

  • Where Do HGTV Chris And Calvin LaMont Parents Live?

HGTV Chris and Calvin LaMont’s parents are currently living in Piscataway, New Jersey. They are both originally from Buffalo, New York, and also have Jamaican roots.
