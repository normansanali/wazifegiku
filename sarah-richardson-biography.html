Who is Sarah Richardson?

Sarah Richardson is a Canadian designer and television personality.

So far, she has been hosting some popular shows in Toronto design series such as Room Service, Design Inc., Sarah’s House, Sarah’s Cottage, and a few more.

Furthermore, she became popular for turning ordinary spaces into magazine-worthy rooms.

Additionally, she has also won Gemini Award for Most Popular Website in 2004.

Sarah Richardson: Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Sarah was born on October 22, 1971, in Toronto, Canada. As of 2023, she is 51 years old.

She is the daughter of Douglas Richardson and Susan Cuddy. Talking about her nationality, she is Canadian and her ethnicity is Caucasian.

Concerning her education, Sarah joined high school in Toronto at Havergal College.

Later, Sarah Richardson completed her graduation with B.A. in Visual Arts from the University of Western Ontario.

Sarah Richardson: Professional life, Career

Sarah began her career as a prop stylist in 1994. Later, she began to host TV shows and design.

She came into the spotlight as a TV personality for turning ordinary spaces into magazine-worthy rooms.

So far, she has been hosting some popular shows in Toronto design series such as Room Service, Design Inc., Sarah’s House, Sarah’s Cottage, and a few more.

Currently, Sarah Richardson is also the Home Design Director of Good Housekeeping magazine.

Awards

Sarah Richardson has won a couple of awards in her career which including Gemini Award for Most Popular Website in 2004.

In addition, she has also received Gemini Award for Best Host in a Lifestyle/Practical Information or Performing Arts Program or Series.
