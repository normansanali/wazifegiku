The Inside Of Kim Kardashian's House Is Filled With Outrageous And Utterly 'Cold' Luxuries That Prov

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 9, 2024

If there's one celebrity with expensive taste, it's Kim Kardashian. The reality star is known for her love of fancy fashion. And whenever she shares details of her Hidden Hills house, which she bought in 2015 when she was still married to Kanye West, fans are totally fascinated by the way that she lives. Kim has a vegetable garden, which is fun to see. But fans don't always like the design decisions that she makes.

When Kim Kardashian posted a video of how she did her house up for Christmas in November 2022, fans didn't agree with her decor choices, according to The New York Post. Kim said, “Look at how magical. In my bathroom, I always have plants out there, and there’s like a whole balcony and you can go through my shower [to] out there." Someone called it a "murder house" and many found it agree.

While not everyone agrees with Kim Kardashian's decor choices, she seems to love her house, which is full of neutral shades and doesn't include any bright colors. What expensive things does Kim Kardashian have in her home?

10 Kim Kardashian Has A Fridge/Freezer With Diamonds On It

People are always fascinated by what celebrities eat. Kourtney Kardashian has followed the keto diet. And Kim Kardashian loves the chopped salad from La Scala that features cucumbers, romaine lettuce, salami, mozzarella, artichoke heats, and sun-dried tomatoes.

While fans would expect the star to have an elegant and sophisticated kitchen, it turns out that it's a lot more over-the-top than people might think. Kim Kardashian has fancy kitchen appliances that set her back quite a lot. According to The Daily Mail, she has a "Swarovski-encrusted fridge freezer."

9 Kim Kardashian Has Four Gold Toilets That Cost $750,000

Kim Kardashian seems to enjoy luxurious things. She always looks like she's dressed to the nines and she never has a hair out of place. So it's really no wonder that she owns gold toilets.

And as one would expect, they didn't come cheap. According to The Daily Mail, Kim Kardashian and Kanye West bought four gold toilets that cost $750,000. Gold toilets are definitely subjective. While some might find them to be too much, others might think that they're super elegant.

8 Kanye West Bought A $600,000 Couch For His And Kim Kardashian's House

According to The Latch, Kanye West bought a couch by the brand Jean Royère Polar Bear for the house that he shared with Kim Kardashian. It cost $600,000, which is definitely more than most people are going to spend on a couch. And it's absolutely more than most would spend on all of their furniture, combined.

The Latch also mentioned that if someone wants to purchase a couch and two chairs from this company, it will cost $1 million. The couch fits into the neutral color palette that Kim favors for her house.

7 Kim Kardashian Actually Has Nine Different Fridges

While it's unknown how much she paid for each of them, the fact that Kim has nine fridges is definitely memorable. Considering her expensive taste and how much appliances can cost, she must have spent a fortune.

According to House Beautiful, Kim posted her fridges on her Instagram Stories, and fans were able to learn more about them. She has devoted three of the fridges to beverages. And one fridge is actually so big that it's a "walk-in fridge," which is pretty wild to see.

6 Kim Kardashian Spent $1 Million On Her Bed

According to Style Caster, Kim Kardashian paid $1 million for a bed by the brand Savoir Royal State. She isn't the only star to purchase one, as the list includes Oprah Winfrey and Madonna. The bed's material is comprised of Mongolian horse, Virgin lamb's wool, and cashmere.

It might seem pretty over-the-top to spend $1 million on a bed... But it makes sense that Kim would be interested in such a pricey item. Everything in her house looks pricey and it's clear from looking in every room that a celebrity lives there.

5 Kim Kardashian Owns Jackie Kennedy Onassis And Princess Diana's Jewelry

Kim Kardashian has also owned some expensive jewelry over the years. According to Forbes, in January 2023, she purchased an Attallah Cross that Princess Diana owned. Kim spent $200,000.

Kim is also thought to have purchased a watch that Jackie Kennedy Onassis once owned, and she spent $379,000 on it, according to Forbes.

4 Kim Kardashian's Jeanneret Chairs Cost More Than $7,000

Kim Kardashian was interviewed by Architectural Digest in August 2022 and shared more about her design choices in her house. She has several Jeanneret chairs in the office in her house. The publication says they cost more than $7,000 and their material is cane and teak.

Kim told Architectural Digest that she is happy with the choices she made for her house. She said. “I love it, I stand behind my decision, and I think they visually look very pleasing,”

3 Kim Kardashian Also Spent Close To $10,000 On A Table

Kim is definitely a fan of beige, gray and white and she also seems to like expensive furniture. According to House Beautiful, she bought a table for her house that cost close to $10,000 and was made by Axel Vervoordt.

The publication reported that Kim shared on Instagram that her children were playing with PAM and that they got some on the table. She said, "So now I’m trying to clean it up, but it’s fully greased. Now my stone table is ruined."

2 Kim Kardashian Has $6,000 Pillows In Her Kids' Play Area

Even the area of the house where Kim's kids play looks neutral. This is definitely a style that Kim favors and now her fans can buy similar items. People reported in 2022 that Kim started a home decor line called SKKN BY KIM. Kim said about it, "When designing this collection, I wanted to bring the monochromatic interior design elements from my home to others."

People reported that Kim bought pillows by the brand Murakami for her kids' area. And they could have cost $6,000. Those are some pricey pillows, that's for sure.

1 Kim Kardashian Even Bought A Side Table For Nearly $1,500

While Kim favors furniture that doesn't feature any color at all, that might not be what most people like. And it's fair to say that not all of her fans are going to like her side table's look... or the cost.

The Sun reported that Kim purchased an onyx side table, and she spent close to $1,500. Kim posted a picture of the piece of furniture on her Instagram account, and it's definitely not to everyone's tastes. And many might think that is way too much money to spend on a small table.
