Cher Net Worth 2024

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 5, 2024

Introduction

Cher, born Cherilyn Sarkisian, is an iconic figure in the entertainment industry. Known for her distinctive contralto singing voice and for having worked in various areas of entertainment, she has been referred to as the Goddess of Pop. Cher’s career spans over six decades, making her one of the few artists to have number one singles in each of the past six decades. This article will delve into the projected net worth of Cher in 2024, her sources of wealth, and her financial journey.

Cher’s Early Life and Career

Cher was born in El Centro, California, on May 20, 1946. She rose to prominence as part of the folk rock husband-wife duo Sonny & Cher after their song “I Got You Babe” reached number one on the American and British charts in 1965. She started her solo career simultaneously, releasing in 1966 her first million-seller song “Bang Bang (My Baby Shot Me Down)”.

Cher’s Music Career

Cher’s music career has been a significant contributor to her net worth. She has sold over 100 million records worldwide, making her one of the best-selling music artists in history. Her successful albums and singles, concert tours, and Las Vegas residencies have all contributed to her wealth.

Album Sales

Cher’s album sales have been a significant source of her income. She has released 26 studio albums, nine compilation albums, and three soundtrack albums. Her most successful album to date is “Believe,” which sold over 20 million copies worldwide.

Concert Tours

Cher’s concert tours have been incredibly successful, often grossing millions of dollars. Her “Farewell Tour,” which ran from 2002 to 2005, is one of the highest-grossing concert tours of all time, earning over $250 million.

Las Vegas Residencies

Cher has had several successful Las Vegas residencies, which have significantly contributed to her net worth. Her most recent residency, “Classic Cher,” grossed over $60 million.

Cher’s Acting Career

In addition to her music career, Cher has also had a successful acting career. She has starred in numerous films and television shows, earning critical acclaim and several awards, including an Academy Award for Best Actress for her role in “Moonstruck.”

Film Earnings

Cher’s film roles have also contributed to her net worth. Some of her most successful films include “Moonstruck,” “Mermaids,” and “Mamma Mia! Here We Go Again.”

Television Earnings

Cher has also earned money from her television appearances. She starred in “The Sonny & Cher Comedy Hour,” which was a popular television show in the 1970s. More recently, she has made guest appearances on shows like “Will & Grace.”

Cher’s Other Sources of Wealth

Cher has also earned money from other ventures, including fashion and perfume lines, real estate investments, and book sales.

Fashion and Perfume Lines

Cher has launched several successful fashion and perfume lines over the years, which have contributed to her net worth.

Real Estate Investments

Cher has made several lucrative real estate investments over the years. She has owned homes in various locations, including Malibu, Beverly Hills, and Hawaii.

Book Sales

Cher has also earned money from book sales. She has written several books, including “The First Time” and “Forever Fit.”

Conclusion

Cher’s net worth in 2024 is projected to be around $400 million. This wealth comes from her successful music and acting careers, as well as her other ventures. Cher’s financial journey is a testament to her talent, hard work, and business acumen. Despite being in the industry for over six decades, Cher continues to be a relevant and influential figure in the entertainment industry.
