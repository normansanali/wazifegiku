What Is The '7th Heaven' Cast Up To Now?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 6, 2024

The family drama 7th Heaven premiered on The WB in August 1996, and it quickly became a major success. The show followed the lives of the Camden family members in the fictional town of Glenoak, California. 7th Heavenstarred plenty of famous faces, and it ended up becoming the longest-running series in the history of the network

Related: Here's Why Jessica Biel Ended Up Leaving '7th Heaven'

The show ran for 11 seasons before concluding in May 2007. Today, we're taking a look at just what the main cast members of 7th Heaven are up to today. From Jessica Biel to Barry Watson — keep on scrolling to see what the actors have been doing!

10 Stephen Collins Isn't Acting Anymore

Let's start off with Stephen Collins who played Eric Camden on 7th Heaven. Today, the 74-year-old actor — who was accused of sexual abuse of a minor in 2014 — isn't active in the industry anymore. In an interview with People, the actor admitted that he committed "inappropriate sexual conduct with three female minors" in 1973, 1982, and 1994. Not much is known about Collins' life today.

9 Catherine Hicks Is Doing Voice Acting

Next up is Catherine Hicks who played Annie Camden in the family drama. The most recent movie the actress can be seen in is the 2014 family movie The Dog Who Saved Easter. 70-year-old Hicks has been more active on television in the past couple of years, and in 2020 she participated in the adult animated horror-comedy JJ Villard's Fairy Tales, in which she voiced Fairy.

8 Barry Watson Is Starring In A Superhero Drama

Let's move on to Barry Watson who played Matthew "Matt" Camden on 7th Heaven. Today, the 47-year-old actor is best known for appearing in family movies and television projects. In 2019, he starred in the family adventure movie A Dog's Way Home, and this year he can be seen in the superhero drama show Naomi which premiered in January.

7 David Gallagher Is Voicing Video Games

David Gallagher who played Simon Camden on the long-running television show is next. Today, the actor is 37 years old, and his most recent projects include an episode of the action drama show S.W.A.T. from 2020 in which he plays Sawyer, as well as voicing Riku in the 2020 video game Kingdom Hearts: Melody of Memory.

6 Jessica Biel Hasn't Starred In A Movie Since 2017

Jessica Biel, who played Mary Camden on 7th Heaven, is next. Since the show, the actress has evolved a lot, and today she is 40 and married to pop star Justin Timberlake with whom she has two children.

Related: Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel's $35 Million LA Mansion Has A 'Confusing' Detail

Jessica Biel's most recent movie is the 2017 drama Shock and Awe in which she plays Lisa, and her most recent television project is an episode of the animated television show Scooby-Doo and Guess Who? in which she voiced herself.

5 Beverley Mitchell Is Acting In Television Movies

Next up is Beverley Mitchell who played Lucy Camden in the family drama. Nowadays, the 41-year-old is still acting and in 2020 she could be seen in the holiday television movie Candy Cane Christmas. Apart from acting, Mitchell also pursued a music career in the 2000s, but she hasn't released a new album in over a decade.

4 Mackenzie Rosman Took A Break From Acting

Let's move on to Mackenzie Rosman who played Ruthie Camden on 7th Heaven. While she was a child when she began acting on the show, today Rosman is 32 years old. The actress seems to have taken a hiatus from acting as her most recent projects are the 2013 movies Ghost Shark and Beneath.

3 Ashlee Simpson Is Focusing On Reality Television Projects

Ashlee Simpson played Cecilia Smith in the family drama, and since then she has established herself as a talented musician. Today, Simpson is 37 years old, and she hasn't acted since the 2016 animated movie Space Dogs Adventure to the Moon.

Related: How Ashlee Simpson Found A New Career On Reality TV

Ashlee Simpson did however appear in reality television shows like Drop the Mic in 2019 and The Bachelor Presents: Listen to Your Heart in 2020.

2 Haylie Duff Hasn't Acted In The Past Couple Of Years

Next on the list is Haylie Duff who played Sandy Jameson on 7th Heaven. Haylie rose to fame as the older sister of former Disney Channel star Hilary Duff, and today she is also 37 years old. The actress' most recent projects include the 2017 television movies The Sandman, The Bachelor Next Door, The Lease, and Hacker, as well as the 2017 television show Real Rob.

1 Tyler Hoechlin Is Starring In A Superhero Drama

And finally, wrapping the list up is Tyler Hoechlin who played Martin Brewer on the long-running television show. Today, the actor is 34 years old and is best known for starring in the fantasy teen drama Teen Wolf. Hoechlin's most recent projects include the 2020 sci-fi rom-com movie Palm Springs and the 2021 superhero drama show Superman & Lois in which he plays the lead.
