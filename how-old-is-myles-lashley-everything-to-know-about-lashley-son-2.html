How Old Is Myles Lashley? Everything To Know About Lashley Son

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Myles Lashley, age 13, is the son of a popular wrestler Franklin Roberto Lashley, who is known by the ring name Bobby Lashley and performs on the Raw brand. Take a deeper look at the life of the wrestler’s son.

Myles Lashley’s father, Franklin Roberto Lashley, is a mixed martial artist and a veteran of the military services, apart from being an American professional wrestler.

Myles was told by his father that he was his father’s hidden weapon. As a result, his father plans to train him as a wrestler in the future. Moreover, his father has also served in the US Army after college.

Myles, mostly recognized for being the son of wrestler Bobby Lashley, is of mixed ethnicity and American nationality, and he follows the Christianity religion.

Myles Lashley Age: How Old Is Franklin Roberto Lashley Son?

Myles Lashley, the son of WWE Champion, was born on 6th July 2008 and is 13 years old as of now. His zodiac sign is Cancer, and he will turn 14 this July of 2022.

Myles resides in Denver, Colorado, America, and was born in Colorado to Kristal Marshall and Franklin Roberto Lashley. His mother is a former professional woman wrestler and a model.

Following his father and mother’s divorce, Myles Lashley was raised solely by his single father. His parents started dating in 2007 and got divorced in the year 2010.

Myles’s father is engaged in many different businesses. He started a healthy smoothie shop in 2007, and he launched the Lashley Network of websites in July 2009. The website includes his official gym, nutrition store, and social media profiles.

Myles Lashley Siblings: How Many Sisters Does He Have?

Myles Lashley has two siblings in his family. He has a biological sister named Naomi Lashley, who was born in 2011 and is the youngest child in the family so far.

Myles has an older half-sister named Kyra Lashley from his father’s side. Franklin Roberto Lashley’s first affair resulted in the birth of Kyra Lashley in 2005. Myles, her half-brother, was born three years after she was born.

Myles’s father, also known as Bobby, is currently single and does not have a partner. He prefers to concentrate on his WWE career and his children’s future at the moment.

Myles Lashley Education: Which School Does He Go To?

Myles Lashley’s personal life has been kept secretive. Thus even though he’s a student, the name of his school has remained hidden.

In the United States, children generally enter grade 8 or grade 9 when they are 13 years old. Because Myles is 13 years old, he has to be in the 7th to 9th standard in his school.

Since Myles lives with his father in Colorado, he is supposed to be attending a school in the same state and most probably in the capital city of Colorado, Denver.
