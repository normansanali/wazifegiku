How to delete pictures on facebook

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

How do you permanently delete photos from Facebook?

How to delete your Facebook photos from the mobile app
  • Open the Facebook app.
  • Go to your photos and tap the photo you’d like to delete.
  • Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner of the photo’s page.
  • Tap “Delete Photo” from the menu that pops up.
  • Confirm your deletion on the next screen.

    • How do I delete photos from my album on Facebook?

  • Scroll down and tap Photos.

    • How do you delete photos on Facebook app?

    From Android App

    Navigate to the photo or video you wish to delete, then tap it to view it full screen. With the photo displayed, tap the three dots at the upper-right corner of the screen., then select “Delete Photo“.

    How do I delete uploaded photos?

    • Why can’t I delete uploaded photos on Facebook?

    You cannot delete photos you did not upload to Facebook yourself. You can remove the tag and that removes the photo from your profile. Additionally, you can ask friends to remove the Facebook photos from their profiles.

    Why can’t I delete old photos on Facebook?

    If you want to get rid of a post or photo that was posted by someone else on Facebook, you can’t delete it yourself. You’ll have to ask them privately to remove it. You can’t remove a photo that others have posted, but you can hide it from your timeline or remove the tag.

    How do I remove a picture from my Facebook profile?

    Click your profile picture in the top right of Facebook. Find the post and click in the top right. Select Delete post or Hide from profile from the dropdown menu.

    Can I hide my pictures on Facebook?

    Click “Only Me” to hide the photo from all of Facebook. If you do not have that option then the photo is part of one of your albums. Click “Edit Album Privacy” instead, click the “Privacy” drop-down menu from the pop-up box, click “Only Me” and click “Save.”

    How do I hide everything on my Facebook?

    Click the Facebook button in the upper-left corner. Select “Edit Profile” at the top of the menu on the left. Click the “Edit” button next to each entry in your profile. Click the “Audience” drop-down menu and select “Only Me” to hide that piece of profile information.

    How do you set your photos to private on Facebook?

    How do I edit the privacy settings for my photo albums on
  • Click your profile picture in the top right of Facebook.
  • Click Photos, then click Albums.
  • Click the album you want to change the privacy settings for.
  • Click in the top right, then click Edit album.
  • Click the current privacy setting (Example: Friends).
  • Click to select a new audience.
  • Click Save.

    • Who can see your photos on Facebook?

    To can edit the privacy settings for your photo albums you’ve posted:
    • Go to your profile and click Photos.
    • Click Albums.
    • Use the audience selector tool under each album to control who can see your album.

    How do I stop someone sharing my photos on Facebook?

    Facebook Help Team
  • Click on the Audience Selector for the photo or album.
  • From the Privacy dropdown menu, select “Custom”
  • Deselect “Friends of those tagged”

    • Why can’t people see my Facebook photos?

    All Facebook users have the ability to restrict who can see their tagged photos and videos (images and videos uploaded by other users, but labeled with their Facebook user name) by customizing their privacy settings. If your name is on this list, you will not be able to see this friend’s tagged photos.
