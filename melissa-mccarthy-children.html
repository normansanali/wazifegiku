Melissa McCarthy Children: Meet Her 2 Children: Melissa McCarthy, full name Melissa Ann McCarthy was born on August 26, 1970.

She’s an American actress, screenwriter, and producer who has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after actors.

McCarthy started her career with stand-up comedy in Los Angeles, and later in New York City and made her first television appearance in an episode of the NBC comedy series; Jenny

In the late 1990s, McCarthy started appearing in television and film but became a household name following her role as Sookie St. James on the television series; Gilmore Girls.

She gained critical acclaim for her performance in the comedy film; Bridesmaids, receiving a nomination for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.

McCarthy went on to star in several commercially successful comedies, including; Identity Thief, The Heat, Tammy, St. Vincent, Spy, and The Boss to mention a few.

She appeared in Samantha Who?, Mike & Molly and also starred in the drama miniseries; Nine Perfect Strangers as well as the musical fantasy film; The Little Mermaid.

McCarthy is a recipient of numerous accolades, including two Primetime Emmy Awards, and nominations for two Academy Awards and two Golden Globe Awards

In 2015, she launched a clothing line (Melissa McCarthy Seven7) for plus-sized women, and in 2016, she was named by Time as one of the 100 most influential people in the world.

McCarthy has been featured multiple times in annual rankings of the highest-paid actresses in the world and has received a motion picture star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame

In 2020, The New York Times ranked her #22 in its list of the 25 Greatest Actors of the 21st Century.
