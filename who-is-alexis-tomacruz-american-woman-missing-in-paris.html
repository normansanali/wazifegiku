Who is Alexis Tomacruz? American woman missing in Paris

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Alexis Tomacruz is an American woman of mixed Filipino and Polish descent who has suddenly disappeared, causing a wave of concern and sparking a massive search to locate her.

Who is Alexis Tomacruz? American woman missing in Paris

Alexis Tomacruz is a 30-year-old young lady who is living in New York City, traveled to Paris at the end of December, and had plans to stay through January, according to reports from her friends on Facebook.

The last time she contacted her friends was about 11 p.m. Friday, Jan. 12. An official missing person’s report has been filed in France.

Alexis Tomacruz stands tall at the height of 5 feet and 2 inches. She has brown eyes and hair and is of Filipino and Polish descent according to the report.

Alexis Tomacruz is taking a French class at Accord Langues and yoga classes and is a graduate of Penn State her friends report, but they don’t know where. She has been staying in the 4th Arrondissement near Rambuteau and the Archives Nationales.

Among the social media posts about Tomacruz, friends say her phone appears to be off and her social media accounts have been silent since about 11 p.m. Friday, Paris time.

Friends have been taking to multiple social media sites to spread the word about Tomacruz and include information for contacting Paris authorities as well as local friends and family.
