Rosanna Pansino Biography

Rosanna Pansino is an American YouTuber, baker, actress and author. She is best known for the cooking series Nerdy Nummies, one of the most well-known baking shows on YouTube.  In the animated YouTube series Broken Quest she  starred as Violet and as The Jet Setter in the YouTube series Escape the Night.  Rosanna has Italian, Croatian, German, and Irish ancestry.

Rosanna Pansino Cookbook

Rosanna wrote The Nerdy Nummies Cookbook.  The book was  released on November 3, 2015.  It includes selected recipes previously featured on Nerdy Nummies. However, some previously featured recipes have been remastered, and two-thirds of the book consists of new recipes that have never been featured on the show.  Rosanna’s book is divided into six chapters and has template barcodes and step-by-step pictures to guide certain recipes.

Rosanna Pansino Youtube

Rosanna stated that her interest in cooking was inspired by her grandmother. She started her YouTube channel after she was encouraged by a few of her professional YouTuber friends. Rosanna began making videos to get more comfortable in front of a camera.

Her baking show began after she filmed the process of making themed treats at a party and gained momentum as her viewers began to enjoy it and started to request more. Since she had never seen a nerdy baking show on television or the Internet, PansinoRosanna decided to create the Nerdy Nummies series. Rosanna’s channel has over 2 billion views and has over 10 million subscribers as of February 2019.
