Jodie Turner-Smith and Joshua Jackson Looked So in Love at the 2023 Oscars

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 22, 2024

We can always rely on Jodie Turner-Smith to deliver the most showstopping looks on any red carpet, and her artful gown at last night's post-Oscars bash was no exception.

The actress looked goddess-like in a bejeweled, Art Decoâ€“inspired gown by Gucci. The two-tone dress featured a red velvet bra contrasted with sheer black mesh at the bust, a diamond-shaped design on the sleeves and skirt embellished with white crystals, and a layered half-moon design on the bodice, also encrusted with crystals. The skirt and sleeves were additionally made up of alternating matte and sheer black lace panels.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

The White Noise star paired the standout gown with black platform heels and diamond jewelry, including black drop earrings and several rings. She also wore a silver nose ring. Her edgy yet glamorous makeup look perfectly complemented her ensemble: a smoky black shadow, an inky cat eye, rosy blush, and a glittery crimson lip.

Shockingly, it wasn't just Turner-Smith's impeccable party look that drew attention as she walked the carpet. The actress posed alongside husband Joshua Jackson, and their affectionate PDA took the spotlight.

Alberto Rodriguez//Getty Images

The Dawson's Creek alum looked handsome in a black velvet blazer, black suit pants, a white dress shirt accessorized with a silky black bow tie, and shiny black dress shoes. While on the carpet together, he sweetly held his hand out for his wife and gazed lovingly at her as she posed for photos. He later wrapped his arm around her waist, smiling at the cameras alongside her.

The couple put all breakup rumors to rest by continuing their adorable PDA after the carpet, as photos from the bash show them looking so in love while enjoying the fete.

Stefanie Keenan/VF23//Getty Images

Turner-Smith and Jackson have been happily married since 2019. In a July 2021 interview, the actor opened up about their family life together with daughter Janie Jackson, telling Entertainment Tonight that there is "nothing" better than "being married to her and having that baby. It's everything."
