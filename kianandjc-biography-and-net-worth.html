KianAndJc Biography And Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, September 12, 2024

KianAndJc is an American lifestyle YouTube channel created and hosted by two guys named Kian Lawley and Jc Caylen. They have a real house that they film for weeks to get viral content. The channel was created on the 27th of July, 2014.

The channel, “KianAndJc” has garnered over 3.7 million subscribers on YouTube with 452 videos and over 730 million total views since creation.

Kian Lawley was born on September 2, 1995, in  Sioux City, Iowa in the United States of America. His family moved to California when he was only five. JC Caylen was born on September 11, 1992, in Houston, Texas in the United States of America. The duo first met at VidCon in 2012. They were part of the collaborative YouTube channel Our2ndLife which was created by Kian Lawley.

KianAndJC Net Worth

Kian and Jc have an estimated net worth of $2.3 million. The duo generates their wealth from advertisements and affiliate marketing programs that run on the videos they post on their channel.
