Biography TimeLine

Erika Jayne is an American singer, actress, and television personality best known for appearing on the reality television show “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills”. Here is a detailed biography timeline of Erika Jayne:

  • 1971: Erika Jayne was born on July 10 as Erika Chahoy in Atlanta, Georgia.
  • 1989: At 18, Jayne moved to New York City to pursue a career in the performing arts.
  • 1990: Jayne began her career as a dancer and performed in several music videos, including those for Madonna and Janet Jackson.
  • 2007: Jayne released her debut single, “Roller Coaster”, under the stage name Erika Jayne.
  • 2009: Jayne released her debut album, “Pretty Mess”, which spawned the hit single “Stars”.
  • 2015: Jayne joined the cast of “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills” for its sixth season.
  • 2017: Jayne released her second album, “XXpen$ive”, which debuted at number one on the U.S. Dance/Electronic Albums chart.
  • 2018: Jayne made her Broadway debut as Roxie Hart in the musical “Chicago”.
  • 2020: Jayne filed for divorce from her husband of 21 years, Tom Girardi.
  • 2021: Jayne and Girardi’s divorce became a primary storyline on the eleventh season of “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills”.
