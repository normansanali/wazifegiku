The top 10 Gym Shorts on the Market for Women

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, September 5, 2024

When it comes to hitting the gym, having the right workout gear is essential. And one of the most important pieces of clothing for women is a good pair of gym shorts. Whether you're into running, weightlifting, or yoga, having comfortable and functional shorts can make a huge difference in your workout performance. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right pair. That's why we've compiled a list of the top 10 gym shorts on the market for women.

1. Lululemon Speed Up Short

Lululemon is known for its high-quality workout gear, and their Speed Up Short is no exception. These shorts are made from a lightweight, stretchy fabric that allows for maximum mobility. They also feature a wide waistband and a hidden pocket for convenience. With a variety of colors and patterns to choose from, these shorts are both stylish and functional.

2. Nike Tempo Running Shorts

Nike is a trusted name in the athletic industry, and their Tempo Running Shorts are a popular choice for women. These shorts are made from a moisture-wicking fabric that keeps you cool and dry during intense workouts. They also feature a built-in brief for added support and a comfortable waistband that stays in place. With a range of sizes and colors available, these shorts are a versatile option for any fitness enthusiast.

3. Under Armour Play Up Shorts

Under Armour is known for its innovative athletic apparel, and their Play Up Shorts are no exception. These shorts are made from a soft, lightweight fabric that offers superior comfort. They also feature a moisture-wicking technology that keeps you dry and cool during your workout. With a loose fit and an adjustable waistband, these shorts provide a customizable fit for maximum comfort.

4. Adidas Designed 2 Move Shorts

Adidas is a well-known brand in the sportswear industry, and their Designed 2 Move Shorts are a popular choice for women. These shorts are made from a breathable fabric that keeps you cool and dry. They also feature an elastic waistband and a drawstring closure for a secure fit. With a variety of colors to choose from, these shorts are both stylish and functional.

5. Reebok Training Essentials Shorts

Reebok is a trusted name in fitness apparel, and their Training Essentials Shorts are a top pick for women. These shorts are made from a lightweight, stretchy fabric that allows for maximum mobility. They also feature a wide waistband and a built-in brief for added support. With a range of sizes and colors available, these shorts are a great choice for any workout routine.

6. Athleta Ultimate Stash Pocket Short

Athleta is a popular brand among women who are serious about their fitness. Their Ultimate Stash Pocket Short is a top choice for women who need extra storage during their workouts. These shorts feature a built-in compression short with a hidden pocket, perfect for storing your keys or small essentials. They also have a wide waistband and a flattering fit that keeps you comfortable and confident.

7. Puma Active Training Shorts

Puma is known for its stylish and functional athletic wear, and their Active Training Shorts are no exception. These shorts are made from a moisture-wicking fabric that keeps you dry and comfortable. They also feature a wide waistband and a drawstring closure for a secure fit. With a range of colors and patterns to choose from, these shorts are both fashionable and functional.

8. New Balance Impact Run Shorts

New Balance is a trusted brand in the running community, and their Impact Run Shorts are a top choice for women who love to run. These shorts are made from a lightweight and breathable fabric that keeps you cool and dry. They also feature a wide waistband and a secure zip pocket for storing small essentials. With a variety of sizes and colors available, these shorts are a great choice for any runner.

9. Sweaty Betty Power Shorts

Sweaty Betty is a popular brand among women who value both style and performance in their workout gear. Their Power Shorts are made from a high-quality fabric that offers great support and coverage. They also feature a wide waistband and a hidden pocket for convenience. With a range of sizes and patterns to choose from, these shorts are both fashionable and functional.

10. Columbia Anytime Outdoor Shorts

Columbia is a well-known brand in the outdoor industry, and their Anytime Outdoor Shorts are a great choice for women who enjoy outdoor workouts. These shorts are made from a durable and water-resistant fabric that keeps you dry and comfortable. They also feature a wide waistband and a zippered pocket for storing small essentials. With a variety of colors and sizes available, these shorts are perfect for any outdoor activity.

Having the right pair of gym shorts is crucial for any woman who wants to perform at her best during workouts. The top 10 gym shorts mentioned above offer a combination of comfort, style, and functionality, making them the top choices on the market for women. Whether you're into running, weightlifting, or yoga, investing in a quality pair of gym shorts can greatly enhance your workout experience. So, choose the pair that suits your needs and get ready to hit the gym in style!
