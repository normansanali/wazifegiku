Who Is The Richest Actor From The Venom Movies?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 29, 2024

The first installment of Marvel’s Venom series, released in 2018, introduced some brand new and exciting characters to the screen (while bringing back some old favorites as well). The film follows a down on his luck, ex-reporter named Eddie Brock (Tom Hardy) as he scrambles to get his life together after being dumped by his fiancé and fired from his job. However, things take an unexpected turn as he is called to covertly investigate a multimillion-dollar science tech company running illegal experiments on humans. As he does this, Eddie becomes infected by the alien symbiote Venom whom he must host within his body. Eventually, the pair find a working rhythm in order to co-exist within the vessel of Eddie’s body.

With the second installment of the series Venom: Let There be Carnage having been released in 2021, the franchise continues to introduce familiar Hollywood faces into the world of Marvel. But whose net worth ranks the highest?

8 Reid Scott - $1.7 Million

https://mobile.twitter.com/mrreidscott

Coming in at number 8 we have Reid Scott. Despite his minor role in the first Venom film, it seems as though his character of Dr. Dan Lewis, is a recurring one as he also appears in Venom: Let there Be Carnage. However, before the comic series first landed on our screens, this critically acclaimed actor was known for his role as Dan Egan in the Emmy-winning series Veep. He also starred as Brendan “Brando” Dorff in the hit TBS Sitcom My Boys. According to Idol Networth, his net worth comes in at $1.7 million.

7 Riz Ahmed - $3 Million

www.instagram.com/p/COSw5ROFn8l/

UK-born actor and rapper Riz Ahmed is an undeniable powerhouse of talent and creativity. With an impressive array of accolades under his belt, this Academy Award-nominated actor comes in at number 7 on this list, with a modest $3 million net worth as reported by Celebrity Net Worths. Ahmed formed part of the cast for the first Venom film and he portrayed the film’s villain, Carlton Drake, a power-hungry entrepreneur with an askew moral compass.

6 Naomie Harris - $4 Million

www.instagram.com/p/CUV1AD4hw71/

At number 6 on the list, we have Naomie Harris. 45-year-old Harris portrayed the character of Frances Barrison also known as Shriek in Venom: Let There Be Carnage. Outside of the Venom universe, Harris has acquired multiple roles in different projects with critical acclaim. For example, she portrayed the role of Paula in the Academy Award-winning Moonlight and even made appearances in multiple Bond films such as Skyfall, Spectre, and the latest installment No Time To Die. Her net worth is reported to come in at $4 million.

5 Stephen Graham - $5 Million

www.instagram.com/p/CU2qKgPtYjs/

In fifth place, we have English actor, Stephen Graham. Graham debuted his role as Detective Mulligan in the series in 2021 during the second installment. Prior to his involvement in Venom: Let There Be Carnage, Graham had been an established actor with several acting credits within film and television dating back to 1990. His most iconic role was in 2006 when he portrayed the character of Combo in the British feature This Is England. Graham received a British Independent Film Award for his work as a supporting actor in the feature. More recently Graham has been a part of various A-list projects such as the 2019 biopic Rocketman and Netflix’s star-studded crime drama The Irishman. His net worth is reported to come in at $5 million.

4 Jenny Slate - $6 Million

www.instagram.com/p/CH_FW8xlRAN/

Taking the fourth spot and just missing out on a spot on the Venom top-three podium is Big Mouth star, Jenny Slate. Slate portrayed the character of Dora Skirth, a quick and intelligent researcher who met her demise due to her betrayal of the film’s villain in the first installment of the series. Before her role in Venom, Slate portrayed a supporting role in the 2017 emotional drama Gifted alongside fellow Marvel alum, Chris Evans. Her net worth reportedly comes in at $6 Million.

3 Michelle Williams - $30 Million

www.instagram.com/p/CUC8s9hNina/

With an impressive net worth of $30 million, Venom’s leading lady Michelle Williams takes the title of the third-richest cast member. In both the first and second installments, she portrays the role of Anne Weying, Eddie Brock’s ex-fiancé and current partner to Dr. Dan Lewis. Much like her character, Williams is talented and incredibly hardworking as reflected in her two Golden Globe Awards and impressive four Academy Award nominations.

2 Tom Hardy - $45 Million

www.instagram.com/p/BqF8ax9g4k6/

In second place on the list is the film series’ leading man, Tom Hardy. Hardy not only portrays the iconic Eddie Brock but also voices the role of his living partner, alien symbiote Venom. Hardy is known for his “tough guy” roles in both film and television, for example, his iconic role as both legendary gangsters and brothers Reggie and Ronnie Kray in the 2015 film Legend and his role in Netflix’s Peaky Blinders. His net worth comes in at an impressive $45 million.

1 Woody Harrelson - $70 Million

www.instagram.com/p/B04Wo-EAzxy/

And finally, taking the crown and first place on this list is Venom: Let There Be Carnage’s villain, Woody Harrelson. The first place spot may come as no surprise to many as Harrelson continues to be one of Hollywood’s most recognizable talents. Harrelson rose to fame due to his breakout role as Woody Boyd in NBC’s 1985 sitcom Cheers. The three-time Academy Award-nominated actor reportedly comes in at a whopping $70 million net worth.
