Golden Globes 2016 Afterparty: Zoe Kravitz Flashes Nipples

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Daring! Zoe Kravitz freed the nipple in a sheer beaded sheath while attending the 2016 Golden Globes Warner Bros. and InStyle afterparty on Sunday, January 10, in L.A.

The Divergent actress, 26, certainly made a statement in the black and gold design by Alexander McQueen. The daughter of Lenny Kravitz and Lisa Bonet accessorized the revealing ensemble with diamond cluster Chopard earrings, a black clutch and black minimalist heels.

Kravitz wasn't the only star to flaunt skin at the bash. Kylie Jenner wore a two-piece rhinestone-embellished outfit by Labourjoisie while Bellamy Young sported major cutouts in a sultry black ensemble by Solace London.

Zoe isn't the only member of the Kravitz family who has revealed an intimate part of her body. Her father inadvertently exposed an entire audience to his penis after accidentally splitting his trousers while performing on stage in Stockholm on August 3.

At the time, Zoe cheekily Instagramed a funny text response to the event with Stephen Tyler's daughter, Chealsea. "So our dads talking about dicks on social media is cool," Kravitz wrote. To which Tyler responded, "What?! Oh no.. Twitter?…Oh god…Hahha #penisgate."
