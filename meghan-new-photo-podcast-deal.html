Meghan Markle Drops a Breathtaking New Photo to Announce Podcast Deal

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 15, 2024

It’s an exciting day for Sussex fans everywhere! Not only did Duchess Meghan announce a new podcast deal that will see her host a future series, she also released a stunning brand-new photo of herself.

In a news post shared to Meghan and Prince Harry’s recently unveiled Sussex.com website, the duchess smiles brightly and leans against a pillar in a portrait captured by photographer and friend Misan Harriman. The snap shows Meghan from the waist up, but we still manage to glean a bit of her cool California style, from her sleeveless gray turtleneck to her signature gold Cartier watch (which once belonged to Princess Diana). She opted for a rosy soft glam look and wore her dark brown hair down and behind her back, styled into loose, large curls.

Misan Harriman

Deadline reported today that the Duchess of Sussex has struck a podcast deal with Lemonada Media, which has produced chart-topping shows like Julia Louis-Dreyfus’s Wiser Than Me, Funny Cuz It’s True with Elyse Myers, Hard Feelings with Jennette McCurdy, and Choice Words with Samantha Bee.

In addition to creating a new series with Lemonada, Meghan will distribute her old Spotify podcast Archetypes through the company.

“I’m proud to now be able to share that I am joining the brilliant team at Lemonada to continue my love of podcasting,” the duchess said in a statement. “Being able to support a female-founded company with a roster of thought-provoking and highly entertaining podcasts is a fantastic way to kick off 2024. Our plan to rerelease Archetypes, so that more people can now have access to it, as well as launching a dynamic new podcast are well in the works. I’m so eager to be able to share it soon, and am overjoyed to be joining the Lemonada family.”
