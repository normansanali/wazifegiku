Jason Kelce And Wife Kylie McDevitt Cherishing A Family Life Together

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Jason Kelce wife Kylie McDevitt was a field hockey player for Cabrini University. Jason got married in an outdoor ceremony at Logan Hotel in 2018.

Jason currently plays in the National Football League for the Philadelphia Eagles. He got drafted in the sixth round for Philly. He played for the Cincinnati during his college days before turning professional in 2011. 

He has had a successful football career ever since his debut. Over the years, he has won one Super Bowl, became a member of the First-team All-pro four times, won the Pro Bowl six times, and was a member of the Second-team All-Big East two times.

Who Is Kylie McDevitt?

Jason and Kylie met through a dating app. Since that Tinder match, have been the perfect shoulder for each other. 

Kylie posted a picture of herself and Jason on November 11, 2015, on her Instagram account saying she was grateful and happy that her future husband swiped right too. 

Kylie Played Hockey

Kylie McDevitt is a former field hockey player and coach. She had posted multiple pictures on her social media account showcasing her career as an athlete before she got hitched to the Eagles center.

She and her team were very devoted during their playing days as they even played during a thundering storm on June 2014.

Furthermore, she even participates in the yearly alumni games at her college. She even went overseas as a player and coach with her CCFH family to Ireland and posted about it on her Instagram.

Kylie and her team won the Colonial States Athletic Conference championship in 2019. She said it was one of her best decisions to become part of the Cabrini Field Hockey family.

Sadly, she is not engaged in field hockey like she used to but her support towards her man has undoubtedly given an athletic touch to her daily life.

Inside Jason Kelce Married Life

Jason Kelce and Kylie McDevitt have been hitched for more than four years.

Jason and Kylie celebrated their fourth wedding anniversary in April 2022. They are now proud parents of two beautiful daughters frequently seen on Kylie’s Instagram.

Based on Kylie’s Instagram posts, she and her husband are fond of their two dogs, Winn and Baloo. There are multiple pictures of the pets on their Instagram.

The couple frequently takes vacations to different places. They took a trip to Oia Santorini, Greece for their honeymoon and posted a picture surrounded by snow.

Jason Kelce and Kylie McDevitt Wedding Moments

The Eagles Super Bowl champion Jason Kelce walked down the aisle with his partner Kylie McDevitt on April 14, 2018.

The couple got hitched at the Logan Hotel in Philadelphia on Saturday afternoon in a beautiful outdoor location.

Jason’s speech on his wedding day moved the crowd of one hundred seventy guests, including the photographer, Sarah Alderman.

Kelce and Kylie’s 120-pound Irish Wolfhound Winnie walked along with the couple. The couple only wanted candid photos from their wedding but was convinced to do some portraits, which can be seen on their social media accounts.

Jason Kelce Daughter Wyatt Elizabeth Kelce

Wyatt Elizabeth Kelce is the firstborn daughter of Kylie and Jason Kelce.

Wyatt was born on October 2 2019, weighing eight pounds and six ounces.

Kylie announced her pregnancy by posting a picture of her dog with a pair of little sandals with the caption, ‘These protectors are due to begin their new assignment September 2019.’

Kylie and Jason frequently take her to games and the children’s park. Wyatt took her first trip to Disney World on January 31 2020. Kylie posted a beautiful picture of her baby with her father on her first birthday.

Wyatt has a little sister named Elliotte Ray Kelce. Jason and Kylie announced their second pregnancy on September 23, 2020, by posting a picture of Wyatt holding an apple with the cation, ‘Another apple joining the Kelce Family tree.’

Wyatt celebrated her third birthday on October 2022 and Jason and Kylie posted her birthday video on their respective Instagram accounts.

Jason Kelce Family Life

Jason Kelce was born to his parents, Ed Kelce and Donna Kelce on November 5 1987 in North Carolina. Jason turned thirty-five last November.

Ed is a sales representative in the steel business, whereas Donna works in a bank. 

Jason has a younger brother named Travis Kelce, an American football player in the National Football League and plays as a tight end for the Kansas City Chiefs. Travis also played college-level football for Cincinnati. 

The dynamic duo of the football brothers is hosts of a weekly podcast called New Heights with Jason and Travis Kelce. The podcast is released during the NFL season every Wednesday.

Jason and Travis said that they had always dreamt of playing professional football and the reason behind starting the podcast was to give their thoughts from inside the game. They share about the new talents on the field and their lives off the game.
