Ray Winstone Role In Black Widow; His Movies, TV Shows, And Net Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 7, 2024

Ray Winstone is an English actor and producer with numerous appearances in movies and TV series like Cold Mountain, Nil By Mouth, King Arthur, The Proposition, Robin Hood, Ever Decreasing Circles, Performance, and Ghostbusters of East Finchley. He, now, works on the post-production of his new movies Sabini and Black Widow.

Ray was a boxer before starting his acting career, with 80 out of 88 bouts.

Ray Winstone Role In 'Black Widow'

Back in June 2019, Marvel confirmed about The Departed star, Ray Winstone's entrance to its comic series, Black Widow.

As Marvel focused its production shooting in London, it exclusively announced that Ray would be joining Rachel Weisz, David Harbour, Florence Pugh, and O.T. Fagbenie in the upcoming superhero movie.

Poster of upcoming Marvel movie, Black Widow (©: wallpaperaccess.com)

Despite the confirmation, his role in Black Widow is not revealed yet. Similarly, Ray has not spoken about his mysterious character in the film.

The Black Widow is set to release on the 24th of April 2020. Likewise, in Italy, the movie will be premiered on 29 April 2020. In the United States, it will release on IMAX on 1 May 2020. 

Ray Winstone's Acting Career And Accolades

We can't deny Ray Winstone is a stunning actor as his long career in the entertainment field shows his consistency and blazing acting credits. During his initial phase in the industry, he starred in the TV shows like You and the World (1975), The Sweeney (1976), and Plays for Britain (1976). 

In 1980, Ray appeared in thirteen episodes of TV series named Fox. Besides, he portrayed several roles in other television series like Family Secret Army (1984), Robin Hood (1984-84), Ever Decreasing Circles (1986), Performance (1991), Ghostbusters of East Finchley (1995), and Vincent (2005-06). 

Also, he starred in films like Noah (2014), Zipper (2015), Jawbone (2017), King of Thieves (2018), Cats (2019), and his first film on action category, King Arthur (2004). He is working on the post-production of new movies like Sabini and Black Widow.

Ray Winstone in his first action movie, King Arthur (©: Pinterest)

Besides acting, he was an executive producer of Ray Winstone in Sicily (2018), Elfie Hopkins (2012), 44 Inch Test (2009), Sweeny Todd, and many others. 

How Much Is Ray Winstone's Net Worth?

Ray Winstone's net worth values $5 million, which he acquires from his acting career. He is not only an actor but also a producer, voiceover actor, and has worked on his self-production projects. 

Before acting, Ray was a boxer. He joined the famous Repton Amateur Boxing Club at the age of twelve and won 80 out of 88 bouts in ten years of a boxing career.

Ray, now, lives in his Essex house, where he refers to the bar as his favorite place.
