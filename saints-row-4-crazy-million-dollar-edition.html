Saints Row 4 gets crazy with a million dollar edition

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 22, 2024

Are you a trust fund kid? Self-made billionaire? Or perhaps a Saudi prince? Well have I got the ultimate game package for you. The just announced “Super Dangerous Wad Wad Edition” for Saints Row 4 will be available for one lucky oil tycoon for the low-low price of $1,000,000.

In addition to receiving Saints Row 4: Commander in Chief Edition, one very wealthy person will receive a Lamborghini Gallardo valued for as much as $248,000 (assuming it is the 2013 model), a Virgin Galactic space flight valued at up to $250,000, 7  nights in the Royal Suite at the Burj-Al-Arab in Dubai valued at $133,000 and a whole host of other goodies that only rich people are allowed to have.

Whether this is serious or not is up for debate as Deep Silver has yet to comment on this pack but you can head to GAME.co.uk if you happen to have a million dollars laying around. And also maybe you can slide a few bucks my way.
