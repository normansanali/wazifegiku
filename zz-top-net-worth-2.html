ZZ Top Band Members History

Throughout their history, ZZ Top has undergone a few lineup changes, but their musical legacy remains strong. The band was originally formed by Billy Gibbons, Lanier Greig, and Dan Mitchell. However, Greig and Mitchell left soon after the release of their first single, leading to their replacement by Frank Beard and Dusty Hill. This lineup of Gibbons, Beard, and Hill would remain unchanged for over 50 years until the unfortunate passing of Dusty Hill in 2021.

After Hill’s death, Elwood Francis joined the band as a touring bassist and eventually became a full-time member. Francis initially served as the guitar tech for ZZ Top, and his close relationship with Hill made him the natural choice to fill the void left by the legendary bassist.
