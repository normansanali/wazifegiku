Tracee Ellis Ross Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Bio:
Tracee Ellis Ross (born Tracee Joy Silberstein on October 29, 1972 in Los Angeles, California) is an American actress, producer, television host and model. She has had roles on the TV shows Girlfriends, Private Practice, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Reed Between the Lines and Broad City. Tracee has won a BET Comedy Award, multiple NAACP Image Awards and NAACP Image Awards for her acting work. She currently stars on the TV series Black-ish as Rainbow Johnson.

Best Known For:
Tracee Ellis Ross is best known for starring on Black-ish.

Personal Life:

Tracee is the daughter of singer/actress Diana Ross. She holds an honorary doctorate of fine art from Brown University.
