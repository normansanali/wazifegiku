Leonardo da Vinci Biography

Leonardo da Vinci is one of the most popular and richest Painter who was born on April 15, 1452 in Anchiano, Tuscany, Italy. Renaissance artist and inventor, polymath, musician and architect who created The Mona Lisa and The Last Supper and sketched the iconic Vitruvian Man. He also invented some futuristic technologies such as tanks that concentrated solar power and adding machines. He also led the way in some significant advancements in the fields of anatomy and optics, civil engineering, and hydrodynamics.

The artist along with He and Michelangelo Buonarroti both defined Renaissance art. each established Renaissance art.

His parents and his peasant Caterina along with his notary Piero Fruosino di Antonio da Vinci did not allow him to marry. He was accused of sodomy in 1476. This led to rumors that lingered for a long time about his sexuality.
