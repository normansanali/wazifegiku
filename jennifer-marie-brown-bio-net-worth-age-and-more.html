Jennifer Marie Brown Is a talented American regional field manager and author who’s birth came in Kansas City, Missouri in the United States of America.

Jennifer Marie Brown Age

Jennifer Marie Brown Was born on the 11th day of May 1977 to Thomas M. Brown who is known widely as a top Entrepreneur being the owner of US 1 dollar shop and Linda Brown who worked as an airport customer service supervisor.

Jennifer Marie Brown Education

She spent her formative years At the St. Joseph in Missouri. The renowned author attended her elementary and high school education in Platte City, Missouri. She then enrolled at the University of Missouri in Kansas after she completed her high school education and studied hard to graduate from the University successfully with good grades.

Immediately after her graduation from the university, she began her career as newspaper writer, having been the brain behind some of the interesting works at Newspaper, a place she served for four good years before releasing her debut novel “Hate List” in 2009 which narrates a shooting incident that took place in one of the high schools in America.

Due to the applause her first fiction received, she was motivated to publish numerous teen fictional books like; Perfect Escape, Bitter End and Thousand Words. Additionally, she published several Children’s fiction like Life on Mars, How Lunchbox Jones Saved Me From Robots, Traitors and Missy the Cruel. These books were soled worldwide and they still trending on bookshops till date.

Moreover, Jennifer began works on women’s fiction under the moniker Jennifer Scott in 2013. Some of her published women’s fiction books include The Sister Season and The Accidental Book Club.

Additionally, She worked for Planned Parenthood Federation of America in Washington, DC as the regional field manager. She educates , supports, recruits and motivate people in the organization.

Jennifer also serve as a consultant for an advocacy. During her free periods, she involves herself in doing series of voluntary works like helping children to read.

Marriage to Jake Tapper

On September 3, 2006, Jennifer Marie Brown exchange marriage vows with her long term boyfriend Jake Tapper after dating for a year and half. The marriage ceremony was held close to their hometown in Kansas City in Missouri.

The union was blessed with two lovely children Alice and Jack Tapper. Their marriage is a good example for other couples since they lived happily without any record of misunderstanding between them.
