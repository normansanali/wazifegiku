The Ultimate Guide: Where to Find Baseball Spring Training in Arizona

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 19, 2024

Baseball enthusiasts eagerly await the arrival of spring every year, not only for the blossoming of nature but also for the beginning of baseball spring training. Arizona has become a haven for baseball fans, as it hosts numerous Major League Baseball (MLB) teams for their pre-season preparations. In this article, we will explore the top destinations in Arizona where baseball spring training takes place, providing a comprehensive guide for fans to catch their favorite teams in action.

  • Phoenix: As the capital and largest city of Arizona, Phoenix serves as a hub for baseball spring training. The city boasts two stadiums: the Maryvale Baseball Park, which hosts the Milwaukee Brewers, and the Camelback Ranch in Glendale, shared by the Los Angeles Dodgers and the Chicago White Sox. Phoenix offers fans numerous opportunities to catch their favorite teams in action, with its vibrant atmosphere and excellent facilities.

  • Scottsdale: Known for its luxurious resorts and vibrant nightlife, Scottsdale also attracts baseball fans during spring training. The Scottsdale Stadium plays host to the San Francisco Giants, offering fans an up-close experience with the three-time World Series champions. Scottsdale's pleasant weather and upscale amenities make it an ideal destination for fans seeking a high-end baseball experience.

  • Mesa: Situated east of Phoenix, Mesa is home to the Chicago Cubs during spring training. The Cubs' Sloan Park is a state-of-the-art facility that provides an intimate and enjoyable experience for fans. Mesa's vibrant downtown area, with its unique shops and restaurants, offers fans an opportunity to explore the city's diverse culture while enjoying their favorite sport.

  • Tempe: Tempe is famous for being the home of Arizona State University and for hosting the Los Angeles Angels during spring training. The Tempe Diablo Stadium provides a fantastic venue for fans to cheer on their favorite team. Tempe's lively atmosphere, coupled with its proximity to Phoenix, makes it an attractive destination for baseball enthusiasts looking for a variety of entertainment options.

  • Surprise: Located in the northwest Valley, Surprise is home to both the Kansas City Royals and the Texas Rangers during spring training. The Surprise Stadium offers fans an intimate setting to watch their favorite players prepare for the upcoming season. With its relaxed atmosphere and friendly community, Surprise provides a unique experience for baseball fans.

  • Peoria: Peoria is another city that offers baseball fans a double dose of spring training action. The Peoria Sports Complex is shared by the San Diego Padres and the Seattle Mariners. This beautiful complex, nestled alongside the New River, provides a picturesque setting for fans to enjoy America's favorite pastime. Peoria's friendly atmosphere and beautiful surroundings make it a must-visit destination for any baseball enthusiast.

    • Arizona has become a premier destination for baseball spring training, attracting fans from all over the world. With its pleasant weather, state-of-the-art facilities, and multiple team options, Arizona offers a unique experience that combines the excitement of baseball with the beauty of the desert landscape. Whether you're a die-hard fan or simply looking for a fun-filled experience, the baseball spring training destinations in Arizona have something for everyone. So, grab your glove, get your tickets, and head to Arizona to witness the magic of baseball spring training.
