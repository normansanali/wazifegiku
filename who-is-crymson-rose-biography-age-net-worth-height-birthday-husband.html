Early Life & Education

Crymson Rose was born in Florida, United States on March 30, 1999, and is now 24 years old.

Her father, Michael Rose, was a football player at the University of Oklahoma and later became a high school football coach in Florida.

Tragically, Michael Rose passed away in a motorcycle accident on November 1, 2005, when Crymson was just six years old.

She grew up in Norman, Oklahoma, where she was raised by her mother, Lori Love. Crymson has an older brother named Damon Rose.

She attended Norman North High School and displayed her passion for sports by actively participating in basketball.

After completing her high school education, Crymson Rose enrolled at the University of Oklahoma, where she is currently a student.

Personal Life

In high school, Crymson Rose was in a relationship with Trae Young, an American professional basketball player for the Atlanta Hawks.

They attended Norman North High School together and even went to prom as a couple. However, they eventually broke up.

Crymson Rose is currently in a relationship with Cedarian “CeeDee” Lamb, an American football player who plays for the Dallas Cowboys.

They gained attention when Crymson was seen sitting beside Lamb during the draft day announcement of his selection by the Cowboys.

Their adorable moments together, including a viral incident where Lamb snatched his phone back from Crymson, caught the attention of social media users.

Career

Apart from being a prominent figure in the world of sports due to her relationship with CeeDee Lamb, Crymson Rose has also pursued a basketball career.
