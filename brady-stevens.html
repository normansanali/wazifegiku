Brady Stevens is recognized as a son of an American professional basketball coach Brad Stevens. 

Brad is the head coach of the Boston Celtics of the National Basketball Association. 

Lawyer mother

His mother’s name is Tracy Wilhelmy Stevens. She is a lawyer. 

She was born on 7 October 1976 in Ohio, United States. Her nationality is American.  

She was born to the late Mary Kay and Albert Wilhelmy. Her mother died in 2004 due to cancer.

She used to play soccer when she was in high school. 

She studied law at Case Western Reserve University School of Law. She is one of the renowned lawyers of Boston.

About father

Brad Stevens, full name Bradley Kent Stevens was born on 22 October 1976 in Zionsville, Indiana, to Mark Stevens, an Orthopedic surgeon, and Jan Stevens, a University professor.  

He has played basketball in high school and University.

He began his coach journey in 2001 from Butler University. He is one of the best coaches among NBA coaches.

He signed as the head coach of the Boston Celtics of the National Basketball Association on 3 July 2013. 

Parents blissful married life

He is one of the lucky guys whose parents are blissfully spending their marital life. 

The couple met for the first time at DePauw College, where they both studied.  

At that time, Tracy was a soccer team captain, and Brad played basketball for that University. 

Their common interest in sports helped them to fall for each other. 

After their graduation, Tracy joined Case Western Reserve University School of Law, and she used to drive five hours every weekend to meet her boyfriend. 

Their long-distance love relationship made their love deeper and committed. 

The couple decided to get married after having love affairs for several years. 

They got married on 8 August 2003 in front of family and friends. 

Tracy supports and encourages her husband a lot. They are a very sweet and decent couple.
