Pokemon Scarlet and Violet Mints

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 26, 2024

Every individual Pokemon has a Nature. This Nature affects two of its stats – by increasing one and decreasing another. The only way to change this Nature is via Mints. Depending on the type of Mint, when used on a Pokemon, it will cause its Nature to switch to another one. As such, Mints are really useful items, and you will want to have as many of them as possible in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet. So here’s where and how to get Mints in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet.

Where to Find Mints in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet

Like many other items in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet, there are several ways to get Mints. The first of these is to simply find the Mints out in the wild on the world map. As far as we can tell, these Mints can appear in just about any location there. So the best method here is to approach and pick up every item on the ground that you see. Do this enough times, and you are bound to get some Mints as well.

The other method is far more reliable and it involves buying Mints from shops. The shop in question is called Chansey Supply. You can find this shop chain in several locations, in major cities in the game. However, to be able to buy the Mints from these shops, you will first have to get a certain number of badges. We managed to get it after we got 6 Badges and defeated the Montenevera Gym Leader Ryme. The Chansey Supply has all of the Mints that are available in the game, so it’s easy to see all of them in one place and decide which ones you want to buy. Each one costs 20.000 Pokedollars, so they aren’t exactly cheap.
