What Is Isaiah Bolden Net Worth?: Isaiah Bolden is an American football player.

He developed a passion for playing American football at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after American football players.

At this time of writing (Sunday, August 20, 2023), Bolden is a football cornerback for the New England Patriots of the National Football League.

He played college football at Florida State before transferring to Jackson State and was later drafted by the Patriots in the seventh round of the 2023 NFL Draft

On May 13, 2023, Bolden signed his rookie deal with the New England Patriots and has since then had an excellent time with the team starting in almost every game.

In August 2023, he hit major headlines following an injury he sustained during the week 3 preseason game between New England Patriots and Green Bay Packers.

Bolden was accidentally knocked to the ground by his teammate in the fourth quarter leading both teams to take a knee while medics rushed to his side.

He suffered an injury after colliding with Calvin Munson (his teammate) after trying to tackle Malik Heath (a Packers player), which left him (Bolden) temporarily immobilized.

The game was later suspended, and Bolden was taken to a nearby hospital for further checks and observation.

New England Patriots Twitter account announced: “Upon mutual agreement of the teams, tonight’s game between the New England Patriots and the Green Bay Packers has been suspended”

However, in an update of events, the New England Patriots cornerback, Isaiah Bolden has been released from the hospital.
