Kelly Simek  KITV4 and Career

Kelly’s career began in Honolulu at  KITV4 More Local News team as an intern. She later worked with the station for 5 months from January 2014 to May 2014. She got had her big break when she was offered a position as a weather anchor and reporter by the KHON-TV station, which is also based in Honolulu.

She currently works for the network, serving as a weather reporter and anchor for the Wake–Up 2day show and also as a Morning News Co-Anchor for the Take2 show, with Kristine Uyeno.

She also works along with Joe Moore, Haward Dashefsky, Brigette Namata, Ross Shimabuku, Chris Latronic, John Veneri, Tannya Joaquin, Mikey Monis, Gina Mangieri, Justin Cruz, Kamaka Pili, Rob DeMello, Ren Clayton, Jenn Boneza, Sara Mattison, Nikki Schenfeld, Manolo Morales, Kimberlee Speakman.

While she was at the university, she studied Geography, Journalism, Meteorology, and Communications to create a sort of personalized degree in Broadcast Meteorology. Simek graduated Cum Laude with a degree in Broadcast Meteorology in 2014. She was also a member of the school’s marching band, Cancer Free Hawaii program, Colleges Against Cancer, and many other societies and clubs.
