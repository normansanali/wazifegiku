Sexiest Star Wars Stars Of All Time Daisy Ridley &amp; More Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

There’s something for everyone in a Star Wars movie. Romance, action, suspense, humor, and a bunch of seriously good looking people. The Last Jedi is set to premiere on Dec. 15, and you can’t even begin to imagine the insane amount of hype surrounding it. Fans are beyond excited to watch Daisy Ridley return as Rey, John Boyega as Finn, and Adam Driver as Kylo Ren. Obviously, we can appreciate their acting talents, but can we be superficial for just one second and focus on their looks?! No Star Wars movie is complete without sexy cast members! Right?!

Over the years, we’ve seen Natalie Portman as Padme Amidala, Carrie Fisher as Princess Leia, and Harrison Ford as Han Solo in the original films. But sadly, even though Carrie re-appears as Leia in The Last Jedi, many audience members will be weeping over her heartbreaking death that happened on Dec. 27, 2016. The Last Jedi will almost mark the anniversary of her death. So, what can we expect from this new highly-anticipated chapter? Mark Hamill will once again return as Luke Skywalker and Oscar Isaac will return as Poe.

As for our protagonist, Rey, she appears to be mastering her Jedi skills! The trailer shows Daisy pulling off some impressive combat skills with a lightsaber in-hand. Could she be training under the watchful eye of Luke? There is also a lot of speculation surrounding Rey’s family and origin story. Will we finally get some answers about her past? Adam may have dropped a massive bomb on Star Wars fans by calling Rey a “princess,” which might suggest that she’s Han and Leia’s daughter. Hmmmm…The plot thickens!
