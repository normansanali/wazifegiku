Technoblade Face Reveal: Everything About The YouTuber

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

Technoblade did have a brief face reveal. For further information, keep following the article.

Technoblade is an American YouTuber who is best known for his Minecraft content. A member of the Minecraft server Dream SMP, he has a friendly rivalry with Dream.

He makes the majority of his videos on Hypixel, a Minecraft server. He is a recurring player in the Minecraft championships (MCC) and is renowned as one of the best PvPers in the history of the tournament. He has participated in the event from MCC 2 to MCC 11 and won twice – MCC 4 and MCC 8.

He has ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder). As a YouTuber, he has 8.01 million subscribers. 

Technoblade’s parents are divorced, and he currently lives with his father. He was an English major before dropping out of college in Chicago, Illinois.
