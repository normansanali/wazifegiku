Photos of the Hunger Games Actor and Hollywood Star Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Stanley Tucci is best known as an actor. Here are some of his best pics in honor of his birthday.

Stanley Tucci is a ‘gem’ of Hollywood. Born on Nov. 11, 1960, the Katonah, New York native grew up with a love of acting. After graduating from SUNY Purchase in 1982 (where he majored in acting), Stanley got his Actors’ Equity guild card by acting as a soldier in ‘The Queen and the Rebels,’ a Broadway play. From there, a legendary career followed – one that saw him appear on screen and stage. He made his film debut in 1985’s ‘Prizzi’s Honor,’ and would go one to be a noted supporting actor in a plethora of films. He also was a respected lead, appearing in films like 1996’s ‘A Modern Affair.’ Many fans will know him for his portrayal of Ceaser Flickerman in ‘The Hunger Games’ series, but he also has made notable appearances on TV shows like ‘ER,’ ‘Monk,’ ‘Murder One’ and ‘BoJack Horseman.’

His first wife, Kathryn Spath-Tucci passed away from breast cancer in 2009. She and Stanley were married in 1995 and had three children together (while also raising the two children from her previous marriage.) He and Felicity Blunt, the elder sister of actress Emily Blunt, were married in 2012. They have two children together.
